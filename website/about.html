Meet the Therapists

We're Nicole and Kiera — friends, colleagues, and the founders of Fern & Feather. We built this practice because we believe therapy should be both evidence-based and deeply human.

👩‍🔬
Co-Founder

Nicole, PhD, LPC

Where Biology Meets Being Human

I'm a full-time college professor with a PhD in Biology and a Master's in Clinical Counseling. That combination isn't accidental — I believe understanding the science behind our emotions helps us heal them.

Before Fern & Feather, I spent years teaching future healthcare providers about the nervous system, stress responses, and the mind-body connection. Now I get to use that knowledge to help real people make real changes.

My Approach

  • Science-based, not woo-woo
  • Trauma-informed with somatic focus
  • Internal Family Systems (IFS) trained
  • Attachment theory specialist
  • LGBTQ+ affirming therapist

Specialties

  • Anxiety & panic disorders
  • Trauma & PTSD
  • Nervous system dysregulation
  • Life transitions
  • Parents of neurodivergent children

"I became a therapist because I know what it's like to feel like your anxiety is running your life. The science gave me understanding. Therapy gave me my life back. Now I help others do the same."

— Nicole

Why We Started Fern & Feather

We met in graduate school and instantly connected over our shared frustrations with the mental health field. Too many practices felt clinical and cold. Too many therapists weren't trained in LGBTQ+ issues. Too much emphasis on symptoms, not enough on the whole person.

We dreamed of creating something different: a practice that was warm and welcoming, scientifically rigorous, genuinely affirming, and integrative in approach. A place where therapists didn't burn out because they had to see 30 clients a week. A place where clients got the time and attention they deserved.

Fern & Feather is that dream realized. The name represents growth (ferns) and lightness (feathers) — what we hope therapy helps you achieve. Growth toward who you're meant to be. Lightness from burdens you've carried too long.

Our Core Values

These aren't just words on a wall. They're how we actually show up — for our clients, for each other, and for our community.

🔬

Evidence-Based

We use interventions that research shows actually work. No trendy techniques without backing. Your time and money are too valuable for anything less.

🏳️‍🌈

Radically Inclusive

LGBTQ+ affirming isn't a marketing slogan for us. It's who we are. We celebrate all identities, relationship structures, and ways of being human.

🤝

Collaborative

You're the expert on your life. We're the experts on therapy. Together, we create a partnership for your growth and healing.

🌱

Sustainable

We cap our caseloads at 12 clients each so we can show up fully present for every session. No burnout. No rushing. Just quality care.

❤️

Compassionate

Warmth, humor, and genuine care aren't optional extras. They're essential ingredients. Therapy should feel human, not clinical.

🔄

Always Learning

The field evolves, and so do we. We pursue continuing education, consultation, and training to offer you the best care possible.
