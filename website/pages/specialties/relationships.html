Relationship Issues Therapy

Rooted and Rising

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

Relationships are where we heal—and where we get hurt. Whether you're navigating conflict, recovering from betrayal, or simply feeling disconnected, we help couples and individuals build relationships that feel safe, secure, and fulfilling.

For Couples

We help partners:

  • Break cycles of conflict and blame
  • Rebuild trust after betrayal or rupture
  • Improve communication and understanding
  • Navigate major transitions (marriage, children, career changes)
  • Rekindle intimacy and connection
  • Decide whether to stay or separate with clarity

For Individuals

Sometimes relationship work happens individually. We support you in:

  • Understanding your attachment patterns
  • Healing from past relationship wounds
  • Developing healthier relationship skills
  • Navigating dating and relationships post-divorce
  • Processing the end of a relationship
  • Building self-worth independent of partnership

Approaches We Use

  • Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT)
  • Gottman Method principles
  • Internal Family Systems (IFS)
  • Attachment-based therapy
  • Communication skills training
  • Conflict resolution strategies

All Relationship Structures Welcome

We work with couples and individuals in all relationship configurations—monogamous, polyamorous, open, or otherwise. We are LGBTQ+ affirming and experienced with diverse relationship dynamics.
