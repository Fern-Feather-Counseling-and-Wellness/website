Understanding Burnout

Burnout is more than just stress; it's a state of emotional, physical, and mental exhaustion caused by prolonged stress, particularly in work environments. It often involves:

  • Feeling drained or depleted
  • Increased cynicism or detachment from your work
  • Reduced sense of accomplishment or effectiveness
  • Physical symptoms like fatigue, headaches, and digestive issues
  • Cognitive difficulties like poor concentration and memory

Our Approach

Regulation First: We help you calm your overactive stress response and reconnect with your body's resources.

Identifying Root Causes: We explore the underlying factors contributing to your burnout—work demands, personal expectations, boundaries, and systemic issues.

Building Sustainable Habits: Develop practical strategies for stress management, boundary setting, self-care, and reclaiming joy.

Reframing Success: Redefine what success means to you, beyond just productivity and external validation.
