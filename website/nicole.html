My Story

I'm a bit of an unusual therapist. Before I became a counselor, I spent years as a college professor with a PhD in Biology. I taught future healthcare providers about the nervous system, stress responses, and the intricate connections between brain and body.

But here's the thing — all that scientific knowledge became deeply personal when I experienced my own anxiety. I knew exactly what was happening in my amygdala, and it didn't help. What helped was finding a therapist who could bridge that gap between understanding and healing.

That's what I offer my clients: the intersection of hard science and genuine compassion. We'll talk about your nervous system, but we'll also talk about your feelings. Both matter. Both are real.

"I became a therapist because I know what it's like to feel like your anxiety is running your life. The science gave me understanding. Therapy gave me my life back. Now I help others do the same."

— Nicole

How I Work

Science-based, somatically-informed, and deeply human.

🧠 The Science Part

I use evidence-based modalities including:

  • ✓ Internal Family Systems (IFS) — Understanding your inner parts
  • ✓ Somatic Therapy — Working with the body to heal the mind
  • ✓ EMDR — Processing trauma without re-traumatization
  • ✓ Attachment Theory — Understanding relationship patterns
  • ✓ CBT — Practical tools for thought patterns

❤️ The Human Part

But therapy isn't just techniques. It's also:

  • ✓ Being real with you — no therapist jargon or platitudes
  • ✓ Bringing humor when appropriate — therapy can be heavy
  • ✓ Collaborating — you're the expert on your life
  • ✓ Celebrating wins — even the small ones
  • ✓ Being LGBTQ+ affirming in practice, not just in theory

What I Specialize In

🧠

Anxiety & Panic Disorders

Generalized anxiety, panic attacks, social anxiety, health anxiety. I help you understand the biology of anxiety and build practical tools for managing it.

⚡

Trauma & PTSD

Single-incident trauma, complex PTSD, childhood trauma. I use EMDR and somatic approaches to process trauma without overwhelming your system.

🔄

Nervous System Dysregulation

Chronic stress, burnout, feeling constantly "on edge." We'll work on regulating your nervous system using polyvagal-informed approaches.

🌱

Life Transitions

Career changes, moving, relationship shifts, becoming a parent. Big changes are hard — let's navigate them together.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Perfectionism & People-Pleasing
