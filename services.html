What to Expect

🧠 Individual Therapy

One-on-one support tailored to your unique needs and goals.

⏱️ 50 minutes
📍 In-person or telehealth
💰 $200/session

Who it's for: Adults dealing with anxiety, depression, trauma, life transitions, relationship issues, or anyone seeking personal growth and deeper self-understanding.

What we address:

  • Anxiety and panic disorders
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Depression and mood issues
  • Life transitions (career, relationships, identity)
  • Grief and loss
  • Self-esteem and identity
  • Stress and burnout
  • Perfectionism and people-pleasing

Our approach: We draw from multiple evidence-based modalities including Internal Family Systems (IFS), somatic therapy, EMDR, CBT, and attachment-based therapy. We tailor our approach to what works best for you.

How long will I need therapy?

It varies. Some people find significant relief in 8-12 sessions. Others prefer ongoing support for deeper work. We'll check in regularly about your progress and adjust as needed.

💕 Couples Therapy

Strengthen your relationship through better communication and deeper connection.

⏱️ 75 minutes
📍 In-person or telehealth
💰 $250/session

Who it's for: Couples at any stage — dating, engaged, married, separating, or anything in between. All relationship structures welcome (monogamous, polyamorous, LGBTQ+, etc.).

What we address:

  • Communication breakdowns
  • Conflict resolution
  • Trust and betrayal
  • Intimacy and connection
  • Life transitions (moving, kids, career changes)
  • Navigating different needs and desires
  • Pre-marital preparation
  • Conscious uncoupling (if separation is the path)

Our approach: We use Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) and Gottman Method principles to help you understand your attachment patterns, communicate effectively, and rebuild connection.

Do we both need to want therapy?

Ideally, yes. But if one partner is hesitant, we can start with the willing partner and work toward joint sessions when ready.

🌿 Group Therapy

Heal in community. You're not alone, and group therapy proves it.

⏱️ 90 minutes
📍 In-person
💰 $75/session
👥 6-8 members

Who it's for: People who want the benefits of therapy plus the power of community. Great for those who feel isolated in their struggles or learn well from others' experiences.

Current & upcoming groups:

  • Anxiety Support Group — Learn coping skills while connecting with others who understand
  • Trauma Recovery Group — Safe space for healing with trauma-informed facilitation
  • LGBTQ+ Process Group — Identity exploration and community connection
  • New Parents Group — Navigate the transition to parenthood together

Format: Groups meet weekly for 8-12 week cycles. Each session includes education, skill-building, and process time. Facilitated by Nicole or Kiera with a trauma-informed approach.

Is group therapy confidential?

Absolutely. All members sign confidentiality agreements. What's shared in group stays in group.

🎓 Clinical Supervision

Support for associate professionals working toward LPC licensure.

⏱️ 60 minutes
📍 Telehealth
💰 $150/session

Who it's for: APCs (Associate Professional Counselors) in Georgia working toward full LPC licensure who want supportive, educational supervision.

What we cover:

  • Case conceptualization and treatment planning
  • Ethical decision-making
  • Trauma-informed practice
  • Self-care and preventing burnout
  • Documentation and clinical notes
  • Licensure exam preparation
  • Building your private practice

Our approach: We believe supervision should be supportive, not scary. You'll get constructive feedback, case consultation, and mentorship from therapists who've been there.

Do you offer group supervision?

Yes! Group supervision is $75/person for 90-minute sessions. Contact us to learn about current group availability.

About Insurance

We are a private-pay practice. This means we don't bill insurance directly. Here's why, and what that means for you:

Why We Don't Take Insurance

  • ✓ No diagnosis required — Insurance companies require a mental health diagnosis to pay for therapy. Private pay means you don't need a label to get help.
  • ✓ Treatment on your terms — Insurance companies dictate how many sessions you can have and what type of therapy is covered. We work with you to decide what's best.
  • ✓ Complete privacy — No insurance records of your mental health care. Your treatment stays between us.
  • ✓ Better care — We cap our caseloads at 12 clients each. Insurance-based practices often require 25-30+ clients to stay profitable.

Out-of-Network Reimbursement

Many insurance plans offer out-of-network benefits. We can provide superbills (receipts with diagnostic codes) that you submit to your insurance for partial reimbursement. Check with your insurance provider about your out-of-network mental health benefits.

Sliding Scale

We believe therapy should be accessible. We reserve sliding scale spots for clients who need them, with priority given to marginalized communities. Contact us to discuss sliding scale availability.
