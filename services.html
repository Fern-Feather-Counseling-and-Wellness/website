Our Services

Evidence-based therapy tailored to your unique needs.

Individual Therapy

In individual therapy, we work together as partners. We're not here to fix you — we're here to walk beside you as you uncover your own path to healing. Think of us as guides who know the terrain, but you are the one who chooses the trail.

Every person brings a unique story, and therapy works best when we honor your autonomy while also bringing in tools that have real science behind them. Here's how we approach this:

Internal Family Systems (IFS) Somatic Therapy EMDR CBT Attachment-Based Therapy Psychodynamic Exploration Trauma-Informed Yoga Mindfulness-Based Approaches

What brings people to individual therapy? More than you'd think. Here are some of the reasons folks walk through our door:

Parenting & Family Dynamics Navigating the challenges of raising neurodiverse children, co-parenting after separation, or healing from your own upbringing
Anxiety & Panic Understanding the nervous system, learning to regulate, and breaking free from the cycle of worry
Trauma & PTSD Processing single-event trauma, developmental trauma, or relational wounds in a safe, supported space
Depression & Low Mood Finding motivation again, exploring what's underneath, and rebuilding connection to life
Relationships & Connection Attachment patterns, communication skills, boundary-setting, and deepening intimacy
Becoming a Healthier Version of Yourself Self-discovery, personal growth, exploring identity, values clarification — whatever "more" means to you
Life Transitions Career changes, becoming a parent, grief and loss, retirement, relocating — transitions bring up so much
Burnout & Professional Caregivers For those in helping professions who are giving so much they're losing themselves

Typical session: 45 minutes | Frequency: Weekly, biweekly, or as needed based on your goals

Couples Therapy

Relationships are hard. Two people, each with their own history, trying to build something together — it takes work. But it doesn't have to feel like a constant struggle.

In couples therapy, we look at the patterns that keep you stuck and build new ones that help you thrive. Whether you're dealing with communication breakdowns, trust issues, intimacy concerns, or just feeling like you've lost your way together, we can get there.

I'm LGBTQ+ affirming and affirming of all relationship structures. Your relationship doesn't have to look like anyone else's to be valid.

Common focuses include:

  • Conflict resolution and healthy argument skills
  • Attachment repair and earning secure connection
  • Rebuilding trust after betrayal or betrayal
  • Intimacy — emotional, physical, and everything in between
  • Navigating life transitions as a team
  • Co-regulating in moments of dysregulation

Typical session: 50–80 minutes (we'll figure out what works for you)

Group Therapy

There's something powerful about being in a room with others who just *get it*. Group therapy combines the expertise of a trained facilitator with the healing power of community. You don't have to do this alone.

We offer several different types of groups, each with a different focus:

Support Groups

Peer-led or facilitated spaces where people share experiences, validate each other's feelings, and build community. Great for those who want connection without a clinical focus.

Therapeutic Groups

More structured groups focused on specific issues — think of these as part group therapy, part psychoeducation. Great for anxiety, depression, or processing trauma in community.

Psychoeducational Groups

Learn the "why" behind what you feel. We teach about the nervous system, attachment patterns, boundary psychology — science-backed content in a group format.

Skills-Based Groups

Want concrete tools? These groups teach practical skills like emotion regulation, mindfulness, communication, and boundary-setting.

Here are some of the groups we're considering — and we want to hear which ones interest YOU:

  • Chronic Pain Support Group — For those managing pain conditions who need community and coping strategies
  • Parenting Support Group — Especially for parents of neurodiverse children navigating the system
  • Caregiver Support Group — For those caring for aging parents, disabled partners, or sick loved ones
  • Anxiety Skills Group — Practical tools for managing anxiety, panic, and worry
  • Boundary Work Group — Learning to say no, set limits, and protect your energy
  • Women's Support Circle — A space for women to explore identity, relationships, and growth
Groups rotate seasonally — not all groups run at all times. Check back often or give us a call to see what's currently being offered.

Typical group session: 90 minutes | Format: Open groups (drop-in friendly) and closed cohorts (sign up for a series)

Workshops & Retreats

Sometimes growth happens outside the therapy room. Our workshops and retreats offer immersive experiences in trauma-informed, body-based healing — think yoga, art, nature, and deep group connection.

Trauma-Informed Yoga

Movement as medicine. A gentle, trauma-aware yoga practice paired with somatic tracking and nervous system regulation. No yoga experience required — just an open mind.

Art Therapy Workshops

Express what words can't hold. Guided art-making processes that tap into creativity for healing. For those who want to explore without the pressure of "being artistic."

Women's Empowerment Retreats

Weekend intensives in beautiful natural settings. Yoga, journaling, group process, meditation, and time to just *be* — away from the demands of daily life.

Couples Co-Regulation Retreats

Intensive weekends for couples who want to go deeper. Learn the neuroscience of connection, practice somatic exercises together, and rebuild your bond.

Boundary-Building Intensives

A full-day workshop dedicated to one thing: learning to protect your energy. Real tools, role-play, and embodied practices for people-pleasers and caregivers.

Nature-Based Healing

Forest bathing, ecotherapy, and outdoor practices that tap into the regulating power of nature. For those who feel most alive outside four walls.

Workshops and retreats are announced seasonally. Some are one-time events, others are part of a series. Check back often or give us a call to learn about upcoming offerings.

Professional Consultation

For fellow therapists and wellness professionals who want support in their own clinical work. We offer case consultation, professional development, and practice-building guidance.

Whether you're an early-career clinician looking for supervision or an experienced therapist wanting to dive into a new modality, we can make it work.

  • Case Consultation — Bring your tough cases. We'll think through conceptualization, treatment planning, and ethical considerations.
  • Modalities — Want to learn IFS, EMDR, or somatic approaches? We can help you integrate these into your practice.
  • Practice Building — For those starting out or looking to grow — business fundamentals, marketing, documentation, and sustainable caseload management.

Typical session: 60 minutes | Format: In-person or telehealth
