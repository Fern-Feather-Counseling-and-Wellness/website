Our Services

Evidence-based therapy tailored to your unique needs and goals.

🧠

Individual Therapy

One-on-one support designed around you. Whether you're navigating anxiety, processing trauma, exploring identity, or seeking personal growth, we create a tailored approach that honors your pace and goals.

We draw from multiple evidence-based modalities — including Internal Family Systems (IFS), somatic therapy, EMDR, CBT, and attachment-based approaches — to build a practice that fits your unique needs.

What to Expect

  • ▸ Session length: 45 minutes
  • ▸ Frequency: Weekly, biweekly, or monthly — your call
  • ▸ Common topics: anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship challenges, life transitions, nervous system regulation, self-worth
  • ▸ Payment: Private pay; superbills available for out-of-network reimbursement
Book a Free Consultation

Why Individual Therapy?

Sometimes you need a space that's just about you — where your story, your goals, and your healing are the center of everything. We create that space.

▸ Tailored to You
No cookie-cutter approaches. Your therapy is built around your unique story, nervous system, and goals.

▸ Deep Healing
We don't just talk — we work with your body, your parts, and your whole self for lasting change.

▸ Your Pace
You decide the rhythm. Some folks want intensive weekly work; others check in monthly. Both are valid.

💑

Building Stronger Connections

Your relationship deserves space to heal, grow, and thrive. We help partners communicate better, repair attachment ruptures, and find practical tools to reconnect.

▸ Attachment-Focused
We help you understand your relationship patterns and create secure connection.

▸ Somatic Tools
We bring your bodies into the room — using co-regulation exercises to shift your nervous systems together.

▸ Practical Skills
You'll leave each session with concrete communication tools you can use at home.

Couples Therapy

Relationships are hard work — and they deserve professional support. Whether you're in crisis, navigating a life transition, or wanting to deepen your connection, we're here to help.

We work with all relationship structures — married, partnered, polyamorous, LGBTQ+. Our approach is affirming, evidence-based, and centered on your unique dynamics.

Session Details

  • ▸ Session length: 50–80 minutes
  • ▸ Typical focus: conflict resolution, attachment repair, trust rebuilding, communication skills
  • ▸ Format: In-person in Alpharetta or telehealth throughout Georgia

Group Therapy

Healing happens in community. Our groups combine psychoeducation, somatic practices, and peer support in a safe, facilitated environment.

Groups are perfect if you're craving connection with others who get it — whether you're working through trauma, learning nervous system regulation, or building relationship skills.

Current Group Offerings

  • Somatic Self-Compassion (Ongoing)
    Weekly practice group combining IFS, somatic experiencing, and mindfulness
  • Attachment Repair (12-Week Closed Cohort)
    Deep-dive into attachment wounds with same-group peers
  • Trauma-Informed Yoga (Quarterly)
    Movement-based healing for trauma survivors
Inquire About Groups

Why Group Therapy?

You're not alone. Group therapy offers the power of peer connection with professional facilitation.

▸ Shared Understanding
There's something powerful about being in a room with others who just get it.

▸ Cost-Effective
Groups cost less per session than individual therapy while offering unique support.

▸ Skill Building
You'll learn tools you can practice at home and in daily life.

Professional Consultation

We offer support and guidance for fellow therapists and wellness professionals. Whether you're navigating complex cases, building your practice, or seeking clinical supervision hours, we're here to collaborate.

All clinicians welcome — no matter your modality or background. We prioritize a collaborative, non-judgmental space where everyone can grow.

Consultation Details

  • ▸ Session length: 60 minutes
  • ▸ Format: In-person (Alpharetta) or telehealth
  • ▸ Who it's for: LPCs, LAMFTs, APCs, social workers, counselors
