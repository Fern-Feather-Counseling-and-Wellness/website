Our Services

Evidence-based therapy and wellness services tailored to your unique needs. Every offering is designed with trauma-informed care at its core.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one support for your unique journey. Whether you're navigating anxiety, healing from trauma, or seeking personal growth, we provide a safe space to explore, heal, and transform.

Our Approach

We integrate multiple evidence-based modalities based on your needs: Internal Family Systems (IFS) for parts work, somatic techniques for body-based healing, EMDR for trauma processing, and CBT for practical skill-building. With Nicole's background in biology, we also help you understand the neuroscience behind what you're experiencing.

Common Focus Areas

  • Anxiety and panic disorders
  • Trauma and PTSD (single-incident and complex)
  • Depression and mood challenges
  • Life transitions and milestone changes
  • Self-esteem and identity exploration
  • Nervous system regulation
  • Grief and loss
  • Stress and burnout

Couples Therapy

Relationship counseling that goes beyond communication skills. We help partners understand their attachment patterns, navigate conflict constructively, and rebuild connection from the inside out.

Our Approach

Nicole is trained in the Gottman Method and brings a trauma-informed lens to couples work. We help partners understand how individual nervous system states impact relationship dynamics, identify negative cycles, and develop new ways of connecting. LGBTQ+ affirming and inclusive of all relationship structures.

Common Focus Areas

  • Communication breakdowns
  • Conflict resolution and repair
  • Trust rebuilding after betrayal
  • Intimacy and connection
  • Navigating major transitions

Group Therapy

Healing in community. Our trauma-informed groups provide connection, shared understanding, and collective growth in a safe, expertly facilitated environment.

Why Group Therapy?

Research consistently shows that group therapy can be as effective as individual therapy for many concerns — sometimes more so. There's unique healing power in discovering you're not alone, learning from others' experiences, and practicing new skills in a supportive community.

Trauma-Informed Yoga

Reconnect with your body in a safe, supportive environment. Our trauma-informed yoga classes combine gentle movement, breathwork, and nervous system regulation techniques to help you release stored tension and restore balance.

What Makes It "Trauma-Informed"?

Unlike traditional yoga classes, trauma-informed yoga prioritizes choice, safety, and agency. You're always in control — no hands-on adjustments without explicit consent, no triggering language, and no pressure to perform. Nicole is a certified yoga instructor who integrates her clinical training to create a truly therapeutic experience.

What to Expect

  • Gentle, accessible movements suitable for all bodies
  • No experience necessary — beginners welcome
  • Emphasis on internal experience over external appearance
  • Integration of breathwork and grounding techniques
  • Choice and agency in every pose
  • Optional integration with individual therapy
  • Props and modifications always available
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
