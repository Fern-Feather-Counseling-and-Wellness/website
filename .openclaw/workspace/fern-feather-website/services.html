Our Services

Evidence-based therapy tailored to your unique needs. We offer individual, couples, and group therapy, plus professional consultation for fellow clinicians.

🧠

Individual Therapy

One-on-one support for your unique journey toward healing and growth.

Individual therapy at Fern & Feather is a collaborative process tailored to your specific needs, goals, and preferences. We work with adults navigating anxiety, trauma, depression, life transitions, relationship challenges, and personal growth.

Our Approach

We integrate multiple evidence-based modalities depending on what resonates with you:

  • Internal Family Systems (IFS): Work with different "parts" of yourself to heal internal conflicts and develop self-compassion
  • Somatic Therapy: Address how trauma and stress live in the body, using body-based techniques for regulation and release
  • EMDR: Process traumatic memories and reduce their emotional charge through bilateral stimulation
  • Attachment-Based Therapy: Understand how early relationships shape current patterns and build secure connection
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Identify and shift unhelpful thought patterns and behaviors

What to Expect

Sessions are typically 45-50 minutes. We begin with an initial consultation to understand your history and goals, then collaboratively develop a treatment plan. Progress is reviewed regularly, and we adjust our approach as needed.

Couples Therapy

Strengthen your relationship through better communication, deeper connection, and shared understanding.

Relationships are hard work, and every couple faces challenges. Whether you're dealing with communication breakdowns, trust issues, life transitions, or simply feeling disconnected, couples therapy can help you navigate these challenges together.

Our Approach

We provide LGBTQ+ affirming couples therapy that's inclusive of all relationship structures — married, partnered, dating, polyamorous, or exploring. Our approach integrates:

  • Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT): Identify negative cycles and create secure emotional bonds
  • Attachment-Based Methods: Understand how each partner's attachment style impacts the relationship
  • Communication Skills: Learn to express needs, listen deeply, and navigate conflict constructively
  • Somatic Techniques: Recognize how stress and trauma show up in your interactions and regulate together

Common Issues We Address

  • Communication problems and recurring conflicts
  • Trust and infidelity recovery
  • Intimacy and connection issues
  • Life transitions (marriage, parenthood, career changes)
  • Navigating different needs and expectations
  • Pre-marital counseling and preparation

Trauma-Informed Yoga

Reconnect with your body, regulate your nervous system, and release stored trauma through gentle movement.

Trauma lives in the body, not just the mind. Our trauma-informed yoga sessions combine gentle movement, breathwork, and mindfulness to help you reconnect with your body in a safe, supportive way.

What Makes It Trauma-Informed

  • Choice and agency: Everything is an invitation — you're always in control
  • No physical adjustments: The teacher won't touch you without explicit consent
  • Focus on internal experience: No mirrors, no comparisons, no "perfect pose"
  • Safety first: The environment is designed to support nervous system regulation
  • Integration with therapy: Can be standalone or complement your therapeutic work

What to Expect

Sessions are 60-75 minutes and suitable for all levels — no prior yoga experience needed. Classes include:

  • Centering and breath awareness
  • Gentle movement and stretching
  • Nervous system regulation techniques
  • Restorative poses for deep relaxation
  • Optional sharing and community connection

Professional Consultation

Support, guidance, and collaboration for fellow therapists and wellness professionals.

We offer professional consultation for licensed therapists, associate professionals, and other wellness providers seeking support with complex cases, professional development, or building a private practice.

Consultation Services

  • Case Consultation: Review challenging cases, get fresh perspectives, and explore treatment options
  • Professional Development: Deepen your skills in trauma-informed care, somatic approaches, or LGBTQ+ affirming therapy
  • Practice Building: Guidance on starting or growing a private practice, from business setup to marketing
  • EMDR Consultation: Case consultation for EMDR-trained therapists seeking support with complex trauma cases

Who We Work With

  • LPCs, LCSWs, LMFTs, and other licensed clinicians
  • Associate professionals working toward licensure
  • Wellness providers (yoga teachers, nutritionists, bodyworkers) seeking trauma-informed training
  • Group practices looking for external consultation

Format

Consultation is available in 60-minute individual sessions or 90-minute group sessions. We meet via secure video or in-person at our Alpharetta office.
