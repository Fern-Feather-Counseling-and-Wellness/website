## 1. Mission, Vision, Values

### Mission Statement

Fern & Feather Wellness Center provides trauma-informed, somatically-integrated therapy that honors the whole person. We combine scientific rigor with compassionate care to help clients regulate their nervous systems, heal from trauma, and build secure relationships.

### Vision

To be Alpharetta's premier destination for trauma-informed mental health care, known for our integrative approach, clinical excellence, and commitment to accessibility.

### Core Values

1. **Safety First** — Physical and emotional safety is the foundation of healing
2. **Science-Informed** — We integrate current research with clinical wisdom
3. **Whole-Person Care** — We address mind, body, and context
4. **Collaboration** — Clients are partners in their healing
5. **Cultural Humility** — We honor diverse backgrounds and experiences
6. **Sustainability** — We model healthy boundaries for clients and staff

## 3. Client Journey

### Phase 1: Inquiry

**Process:**
1. Potential client submits inquiry via website, phone, or email
2. Administrative coordinator responds within 24 business hours
3. Initial screening call scheduled (15 minutes, free)
4. Screening assesses: fit, urgency, insurance, preferences

**Decision Points:**
- If crisis/urgent → Provide crisis resources, schedule ASAP if appropriate
- If not a fit → Provide referrals
- If fit → Schedule intake

### Phase 2: Intake

**Before First Session:**
- Intake forms sent via secure portal (48 hours before)
- Insurance verified (if applicable)
- Payment method on file
- Room assigned

**First Session (60-90 minutes):**
- Review informed consent
- Collect history
- Establish goals
- Assess safety
- Schedule ongoing sessions

**Documentation:**
- Intake assessment completed
- Treatment plan drafted
- Diagnosis assigned (if appropriate)
- Informed consent signed

### Phase 3: Ongoing Treatment

**Standard Session:** 50-55 minutes
**Frequency:** Weekly or biweekly (determined collaboratively)

**Session Structure:**
1. Check-in (5 min)
2. Review of week/progress (5-10 min)
3. Main therapeutic work (30-35 min)
4. Closing/integration (5-10 min)

**Between Sessions:**
- Client has access to portal for messaging (non-urgent)
- Crisis line provided for emergencies
- Resources shared as appropriate

### Phase 4: Termination

**Planned Termination:**
- Discussed collaboratively when goals met
- Gradual reduction in frequency
- Relapse prevention planning
- Open door for return

**Unplanned Termination:**
- Document reason if known
- Provide referrals if appropriate
- Offer closure session if client willing

## 4. Clinical Operations

### Documentation Standards

**Required Notes:**
- Intake assessment (initial)
- Treatment plan (initial, updated every 90 days)
- Progress notes (every session)
- Termination summary (at discharge)

**Note Timeline:**
- Progress notes: Within 24 hours of session
- Treatment plan updates: Every 90 days
- Incident reports: Within 24 hours

**Note Content (SOAP format):**
- **Subjective:** Client report
- **Objective:** Observable data
- **Assessment:** Clinical formulation
- **Plan:** Next steps

### Safety Protocols

**Suicide Risk Assessment:**
- Assessed at intake and when indicated
- Columbia Suicide Severity Rating Scale used
- Safety plan created for elevated risk
- Supervisor consulted for high risk

**Mandatory Reporting:**
- Child abuse/neglect
- Elder abuse/neglect
- Dependent adult abuse
- Imminent danger to others

**Documentation:** Report made within required timeframe, documented in file

### Clinical Supervision

**Individual Supervision:**
- Frequency: Weekly for associates, monthly for independently licensed
- Format: Case review, skill development, self-care check
- Documentation: Supervision log maintained

**Group Consultation:**
- Frequency: Monthly
- Format: Case presentation, peer support
- Attendance: Required for all clinical staff

## 5. Administrative Operations

### Scheduling

**Standard Hours:**
- Monday-Thursday: 9am-8pm
- Friday: 9am-5pm
- Saturday: 10am-2pm (limited)

**Booking:**
- Minimum 24 hours advance booking
- 48-hour cancellation policy
- Waitlist maintained for popular times

**Cancellations:**
- 48+ hours: No charge, rescheduled
- 24-48 hours: 50% session fee
- <24 hours: Full session fee
- No-show: Full session fee

### Communication Standards

**Response Times:**
- Phone: Within 24 business hours
- Email: Within 24 business hours
- Portal messages: Within 48 business hours
- Crisis: Immediate (crisis line)

**Tone:** Warm, professional, clear

### Office Management

**Opening Procedures:**
- Check messages
- Review schedule
- Prepare rooms
- Check supplies

**Closing Procedures:**
- Secure files
- Lock rooms
- Set alarm
- Check tomorrow's schedule

## 9. Emergency Procedures

### Client Crisis

**During Business Hours:**
1. Assess immediate risk
2. Create safety plan
3. Contact emergency contact if appropriate
4. Document thoroughly
5. Follow up within 24 hours

**After Hours:**
- Crisis line provided to all clients
- Georgia Crisis & Access Line: 1-800-715-4225
- 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
- 911 for imminent danger

### Office Emergency

**Medical Emergency:**
1. Call 911
2. Provide first aid if trained
3. Clear area
4. Document incident

**Security Threat:**
1. Prioritize safety
2. Call 911 if needed
3. Follow facility protocol
4. Document and debrief

**Natural Disaster:**
- Follow local emergency guidance
- Notify clients of closures
- Secure premises
- Resume when safe
