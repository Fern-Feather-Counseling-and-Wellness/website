Crisis Resources

Important: Fern & Feather Counseling is not equipped to handle life-threatening emergencies. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please use the resources below right away.

For Immediate Life-Threatening Emergencies

Call 911

Police, fire, and ambulance response. Available 24/7.

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or text 988

Free, confidential support 24/7. Available nationwide.

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free crisis support via text message.

The Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ Youth)

1-866-488-7386

Crisis support specifically for LGBTQ+ youth. Text START to 678-678.

National Sexual Assault Hotline

1-800-656-4673

Free, confidential support for survivors of sexual assault. Available 24/7.

Georgia Crisis & Access Line

1-800-715-4225

Georgia-specific mental health crisis support. Available 24/7.
