# Intake Workflow & Automation Plan

## Objective

To design and implement a seamless, efficient, and client-centered intake process for Fern & Feather Wellness Center that automates administrative tasks, improves client experience, and ensures all necessary information is collected accurately and securely.

## Key Components of the Intake Process

1.  **Client Inquiry & Scheduling:**
    *   Initial contact via website form or direct inquiry.
    *   Automated response with preliminary information (e.g., services, pricing, Nicole's philosophy).
    *   Link to online scheduler.

2.  **Online Scheduling & Initial Data Collection:**
    *   Clients book consultation or first session via an integrated scheduling tool (e.g., SimplePractice, Acuity Scheduling).
    *   During booking, clients provide basic contact information and reason for seeking services.

3.  **Secure Client Portal Access:**
    *   Upon booking confirmation, clients receive credentials to a secure client portal.
    *   Portal to host all intake forms.

4.  **Intake Forms Package:**
    *   **Intake Questionnaire:** Detailed history (medical, psychological, social, family), current concerns, goals, therapy history, and client preferences.
    *   **Informed Consent:** Full explanation of services, confidentiality, fees, cancellation policy, and client rights.
    *   **HIPAA/Privacy Policy Acknowledgement:** Compliance with privacy regulations.
    *   **Telehealth Consent (if applicable):** Specific consent for virtual sessions.
    *   **Insurance & Billing Information:** Collected if applicable (though Fern & Feather is private-pay, this section might be for verification or superbill generation if needed later).
    *   **Release of Information (ROI) Form:** If needed for coordination of care.

5.  **Pre-Session Review by Clinician (Nicole/Kiera):**
    *   Review of submitted forms prior to the first session.
    *   Identification of key themes, potential safety concerns, and areas to focus on.

6.  **First Session:**
    *   Clinician-guided discussion to confirm and elaborate on intake information.
    *   Building therapeutic rapport.

7.  **Post-Session Follow-up:**
    *   Automated email confirming next steps and scheduling the following session.

## Automation Strategy

*   **Website Integration:** Seamless connection between website inquiry forms, scheduling software, and client portal.
*   **Client Portal:** A secure, HIPAA-compliant platform (e.g., SimplePractice, Luminello, TherapyNotes) will be essential.
*   **Email Automation:** Drip campaigns for reminders, new client onboarding, and follow-ups.
*   **Document Management:** Secure storage and retrieval of client forms.

## Phases of Implementation

1.  **Research & Selection:** Identify and choose a suitable client management software (CRM/EHR) that supports intake automation, scheduling, and a client portal.
2.  **Form Design & Digitization:** Create/refine all necessary intake forms, ensuring they are HIPAA-compliant and user-friendly for digital completion.
3.  **Workflow Mapping:** Visually map out the end-to-end client journey from initial inquiry to the first session.
4.  **Software Integration:** Configure the chosen software to automate the process: form distribution, scheduling, reminders, and secure portal access.
5.  **Testing:** Conduct thorough testing with mock client data to ensure all steps function correctly and securely.
6.  **Documentation:** Document the final workflow and training materials for clinicians.
