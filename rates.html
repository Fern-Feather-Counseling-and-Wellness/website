Why We Don't Take Insurance

Do you take insurance?
Nope — and that's by design. Insurance companies aren't in the business of your wellbeing; they're in the business of profit. When insurance gets involved, they require diagnoses that become permanent records, decide what "counts" as therapy (usually only crisis management, not actual growth), and limit how much care you can receive.

We chose private-pay so your records stay actually private, you don't need a label to get help, and we can focus on what you actually need — not what some insurer's spreadsheet says you should have.

We know paying out of pocket is a real investment. We're committed to working with you to make therapy accessible — during your free consultation, we're happy to talk through options and find an arrangement that fits your situation.
