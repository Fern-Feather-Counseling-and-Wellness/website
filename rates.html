Rates & Fees

Transparent pricing. No surprise bills.

Our Fees (Private Pay)

We are a private-pay practice, which means we don't accept insurance. This allows us to maintain your privacy, offer longer sessions, and focus on your needs rather than insurance-imposed limits. We provide superbills for out-of-network reimbursement if you'd like to pursue that option.

Individual Therapy

$135–$180 • 45 minutes

Couples Therapy

$210–$235 • 50–80 minutes

Group Therapy

$60–$75 • 90 minutes

Professional Consultation

$190 • 60 minutes

Sliding scale spots may be available for those with financial need. During your free consultation, we can discuss options.

Why No Insurance?

We chose to go out-of-network for good reasons:

  • ✓ Privacy — No diagnosis on your permanent record
  • ✓ Flexibility — We decide session length, not an insurer
  • ✓ Control — You choose how often to meet
  • ✓ Focus — We treat you, not a diagnosis code

We can provide superbills for you to submit to your insurance for potential reimbursement. Check with your plan about out-of-network benefits.
