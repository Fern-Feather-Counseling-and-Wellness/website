My Story

I'm a bit of an unusual therapist. Before I became a counselor, I spent years as a college professor with a PhD in Biology. I taught future healthcare providers about the nervous system, stress responses, and the intricate connections between brain and body. I still teach — I love it, and I love working with young adults who are navigating their own big questions about life.

All that scientific knowledge became deeply personal when I started to process my own trauma and anxiety — and when I learned that my child is neurodivergent. I knew exactly what was happening in the nervous system, but knowing wasn't enough. I wanted to understand my daughter, to understand myself, and what I discovered was that therapy is incredibly powerful.

That's what drew me to become a clinician. I went back to school for counseling, and now I help others the way therapy helped me — at the intersection of hard science and genuine compassion. I still teach healthcare professionals, because I believe the best therapists never stop learning. Whether you're an individual struggling with anxiety, a couple trying to reconnect, or a parent navigating the complexities of raising a neurodivergent kid, we'll use evidence-based approaches that actually work.

A Holistic Approach to Healing

Because you are more than just your symptoms.

I believe in treating the whole person — not just the diagnosis. Your mind, body, and emotional world are all connected, and healing happens when we address all of these parts with evidence-based approaches tailored to your unique needs.

Modalities I Draw From

As a trained scientist, evidence-based practice is paramount to me. These are just some of the approaches I use — we'll find what works for your unique needs.

  • 🧠 IFS (Internal Family Systems) — Understanding your inner parts and how they work together.
  • 🌱 Somatic Therapies — Working with the body to release stored trauma and activate healing.
  • 🔄 ERP (Exposure & Response Prevention) — Gold-standard treatment for OCD and anxiety.
  • 💞 Attachment Theory — Understanding how relationships shape your patterns and healing through connection.
  • 💑 Gottman Method — Research-based couples therapy for deeper understanding and reconnection.
  • ⚖️ DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy) — Building distress tolerance, emotional regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness.
  • 🧩 CBT & ACT (Cognitive Behavioral & Acceptance Commitment Therapy) — Practical tools for managing thoughts and building a meaningful life.

And more — these are just some of the tools in my toolkit. Not sure which approach is right for you? We'll figure it out together. Your healing, your way.
