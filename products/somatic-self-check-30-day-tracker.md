# The Somatic Self-Check
## 30 Days of Nervous System Tracking

**A Daily Practice for Building Body Awareness & Regulation**

---

## How to Use This Tracker

**The Goal:** Build somatic awareness by checking in with your body daily. Over 30 days, you'll start to notice patterns in your nervous system states and what influences them.

**Time Required:** 2-3 minutes per day

**What You'll Track:**
- Morning baseline state
- Evening wind-down state  
- Triggers or resources that shifted your state
- Somatic practices used
- Sleep quality

## Nervous System States Quick Reference

### Ventral Vagal (Safe & Social)
**Body cues:** Relaxed face, easy breathing, soft belly, warm hands
**Capacity:** Connection, creativity, curiosity, play
**When you're here:** Great for relationships, learning, trying new things

### Sympathetic (Mobilized)
**Body cues:** Tension, fast heartbeat, shallow breathing, restlessness
**States:** Anxious, angry, frustrated, panicked, excited
**Need:** Discharge energy, movement, boundaries, safety

### Dorsal Vagal (Shutdown)
**Body cues:** Numb, heavy, frozen, foggy, collapsed, disconnected
**States:** Depressed, exhausted, dissociated, hopeless
**Need:** Gentle warmth, small movements, co-regulation, rest

### Mixed States
**Hyperaroused dorsal:** Panic (high energy + collapse)
**Hypoaroused sympathetic:** Rigid control (frozen but wired)

**Remember:** These are adaptive responses, not failures. Your nervous system is trying to protect you.

## Regulation Strategies Reminder

### For High Activation (Sympathetic)
- [ ] Shake out arms and legs
- [ ] Push against a wall
- [ ] Cold water on face/wrists
- [ ] Slow exhale (longer than inhale)
- [ ] Orient to the room (name 5 things you see)
- [ ] Ground through feet (press into floor)
- [ ] Change temperature (cooler room, cold drink)
- [ ] Vigorous movement (run, dance, jump)

### For Low Activation (Dorsal)
- [ ] Warmth (blanket, bath, warm drink)
- [ ] Gentle stretching
- [ ] Rocking or swaying
- [ ] Pet an animal or soft texture
- [ ] Small, simple task completion
- [ ] Co-regulation (trusted person's presence)
- [ ] Music with strong rhythm
- [ ] Sunlight or bright light

### For Connection (Ventral Vagal Support)
- [ ] Humming or singing
- [ ] Deep slow breathing
- [ ] Facial relaxation (soften jaw, eyes)
- [ ] Social engagement (safe person)
- [ ] Laughter
- [ ] Loving-kindness phrases
- [ ] Orient to beauty/pleasure
