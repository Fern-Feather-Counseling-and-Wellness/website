## Usage Guidelines for Therapists

### How to Use These Tools in Practice

**1. Initial Session:**
- Introduce 1-2 tools that match client needs
- Explain purpose and how to use
- Send home with printed copies

**2. Between Sessions:**
- Assign specific tracking tools as homework
- Review completed trackers in next session
- Look for patterns and insights together

**3. Crisis Support:**
- Ensure every client has Crisis Safety Plan
- Review and update regularly
- Keep copy in client file (with permission)

**4. Group Settings:**
- Grounding cards work well in group therapy
- Weekly reflection can be group discussion prompt
- Window of tolerance useful for psychoeducation

### Printing Recommendations

- **Daily trackers:** Print 30 copies for monthly use
- **Grounding cards:** Print on cardstock, laminate for durability
- **Crisis plans:** Print on bright colored paper for visibility
- **Self-care menu:** Keep in visible location (fridge, mirror)

### Customization

These templates can be:
- Branded with your practice logo
- Modified for specific client populations
- Translated for non-English speakers
- Digitized for tablet/phone use
