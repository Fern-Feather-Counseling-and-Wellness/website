## Tool 1: Daily Nervous System Tracker

**Purpose:** Track arousal levels throughout the day to identify patterns

**Design Specs:**
- Size: US Letter & A4
- Layout: Vertical daily tracker
- Sections:
  - Morning check-in (time, arousal 0-10, notes)
  - 4x hourly check-ins (time, arousal, trigger/event, regulation tool used)
  - Evening reflection (high/low of day, patterns noticed, tomorrow's intention)
- Visual: Color-coded zones (green=regulated, yellow=elevated, red=overwhelmed, blue=low energy)
- Fern & Feather branding, subtle botanical elements

**Usage Instructions (included):**
"Track your nervous system for 7 days to identify patterns. Rate your arousal level 0-10 throughout the day. Notice what triggers shifts and what helps you regulate."

**Pricing:** $4 (individual) / included in Complete Toolkit

## Tool 2: Window of Tolerance Worksheet

**Purpose:** Visualize and understand personal window of tolerance

**Design Specs:**
- Size: US Letter & A4
- Layout: Horizontal with three zones
- Sections:
  - **Hyperarousal Zone (red):** "Fight/Flight" — list your signs (racing heart, racing thoughts, irritability, etc.)
  - **Window of Tolerance (green):** "Regulated" — list your signs (calm, focused, connected, present)
  - **Hypoarousal Zone (blue):** "Freeze/Shut Down" — list your signs (numb, disconnected, exhausted, can't think)
  - **My Triggers:** Space to list top 5 triggers for each zone
  - **My Regulation Tools:** Space to list what brings you back to window
- Visual: Thermometer-style graphic showing the three zones

**Usage Instructions:**
"Map your personal window of tolerance. Everyone's window is different. Knowing yours helps you recognize early warning signs and choose the right regulation tools."

**Pricing:** $5 (individual) / included in Complete Toolkit

## Tool 3: Somatic Grounding Cards

**Purpose:** Portable grounding techniques for acute moments

**Design Specs:**
- Size: 3.5" x 2" cards (business card size), 8 cards per sheet
- Double-sided:
  - **Front:** Technique name + simple icon
  - **Back:** Step-by-step instructions
- Cards included:
  1. **5-4-3-2-1 Senses** — Ground through sight, sound, touch, smell, taste
  2. **Physiological Sigh** — Double inhale, long exhale
  3. **Cold Water Splash** — Splash cold water on face or hold ice
  4. **Self-Hug** — Cross arms, squeeze, hold
  5. **Wall Press** — Push against wall with hands or back
  6. **Orienting** — Look around, name 5 things you see
  7. **Belly Breathing** — Hand on belly, breathe into hand
  8. **Emergency Contact** — Space to write your person
- Print on cardstock, cut, laminate optional
- Hole punch option for keyring

**Usage Instructions:**
"Cut out these cards and keep them in your wallet, car, or desk. When you feel activated, pull a card and follow the steps."

**Pricing:** $5 (individual) / included in Complete Toolkit

## Tool 10: Breathwork Menu

**Purpose:** Quick reference for different breathing techniques

**Design Specs:**
- Size: US Letter (poster) + wallet card (3.5" x 2")
- Layout: 5 techniques with icons
- Content:
  1. **Physiological Sigh** — For acute stress
     - Double inhale through nose, long exhale through mouth
  2. **Box Breathing** — For focus
     - Inhale 4, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4
  3. **4-7-8 Breathing** — For sleep
     - Inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8
  4. **Coherent Breathing** — For daily regulation
     - 5-6 breaths per minute, equal in/out
  5. **Resonant Breathing** — For deep regulation
     - Find your personal resonance (usually 3-7/min)
- Visual: Simple diagrams showing breath patterns

**Usage Instructions:**
"Different situations call for different breath techniques. Keep this handy and experiment to find what works for you."

**Pricing:** $4 (individual) / included in Complete Toolkit
