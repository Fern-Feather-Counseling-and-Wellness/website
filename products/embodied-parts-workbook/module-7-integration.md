## Part 2: Recognizing Patterns and Themes

### Common Patterns in Parts Work

As you review your journey, you may notice recurring patterns:

**The Same Part, Different Contexts**
- A part that shows up at work also shows up in relationships
- The trigger changes, but the part is the same
- Example: A perfectionist part that activates with deadlines also activates with partner's expectations

**The Part Chain Reaction**
- One part triggers another, which triggers another
- Example: Anxious part → Controlling part → Angry part → Shamed part
- Understanding the chain helps interrupt it earlier

**The Somatic Signature Family**
- Certain sensations cluster together
- Example: Tight jaw + shallow breathing + racing thoughts = perfectionist part activated
- Learning your signatures helps with early recognition

**The Self-Energy Conditions**
- Self is more accessible in certain conditions
- Example: After exercise, in nature, first thing in morning, after meditation
- You can use these conditions strategically

## Part 3: Creating Your Ongoing Practice

### The Three-Tier Practice Model

Sustainable practice happens at three levels:

**Tier 1: Daily Micro-Practices (5-10 minutes total)**
- Morning Self check-in (2 minutes)
- Mid-day anchor use (30 seconds, 3x)
- Evening reflection (3 minutes)

**Tier 2: Weekly Deeper Practices (20-30 minutes, 2-3x per week)**
- One full somatic IFS exercise
- Journaling
- Parts dialogue

**Tier 3: Monthly Integration (1-2 hours)**
- Review your patterns
- Work with a challenging part
- Update your practice plan

## Part 5: Sustaining the Work

### Signs of Progress (Even When It Doesn't Feel Like It)

Parts work can feel like two steps forward, one step back. Here are signs you're making progress—even when it doesn't feel like it:

**Increased Awareness**
- You notice parts faster than before
- You can name what's happening while it's happening
- You have moments of "Oh, that's my [part name]"

**Shorter Reactivity**
- The time between trigger and awareness is shrinking
- You catch yourself mid-reaction sometimes
- You can pause before responding more often

**More Choice**
- You feel less like a victim of your reactions
- You can sometimes choose your response
- Parts don't feel as "in control"

**Self-Energy Moments**
- You have moments of genuine calm, curiosity, or compassion
- Even brief moments count
- These moments may be fleeting but they're real

**Better Relationships**
- People may comment that you seem different
- You may notice you're less reactive with certain people
- Repair happens more easily

**Body Changes**
- You may notice less chronic tension
- Your breath may be more accessible
- You may feel more "in" your body

### Common Setbacks and How to Handle Them

**"I Was Doing Better and Now I'm Worse"**
- This is often a sign that deeper material is surfacing
- It means you have enough safety to touch harder stuff
- Go back to basics: grounding, small steps, support

**"I Can't Access Self Anymore"**
- Parts may be activated by life stressors
- Self is always there—sometimes parts are just louder
- Focus on relationship with parts rather than forcing Self

**"This Is Taking Too Long"**
- Parts work is slow because it needs to be
- You're rewiring decades of patterns
- Compare yourself to where you started, not to an ideal

**"I Need to Do This Perfectly"**
- That's a part talking (probably a perfectionist)
- There is no perfect parts work
- Showing up imperfectly is still showing up
