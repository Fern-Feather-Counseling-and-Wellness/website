# Fern & Feather Digital Product Roadmap
## Paid Digital Products: B2C (Clients) + B2B (Therapists)

---

## Product Strategy Overview

**Tier 1: Entry-Level Workbooks ($27-$47)**
- Low barrier to entry
- Highly specific outcomes
- PDF + printable components
- Self-guided completion

**Tier 2: Comprehensive Courses ($97-$197)**
- Video + workbook hybrid
- Structured curriculum (4-8 modules)
- Community bonus or bonus worksheets
- Transformation-focused

**Tier 3: Practitioner Toolkits ($147-$297)**
- For licensed therapists/coaches
- Done-for-you materials
- CE credit-eligible where possible
- Professional licensing

## Technical Requirements

**Delivery Platform:**
- Teachable or Kajabi (hosting + payment + email)
- Option to start with Gumroad (simpler, lower cost, less flexibility)
- Consider: Do you want tiers or paid communities?

**Payment Processing:**
- Stripe (primary)
- PayPal (backup)
- State sales tax considerations

**Format Decisions:**
- Workbooks: PDF (interactive where possible) + printable
- Courses: Video hosting on platform with download option for audio
- Toolkits: PDF only (no video)

**Security:**
- Watermark or buyer info on therapist toolkits (reduce unauthorized sharing)
- Terms of use specifying single-user license
- No refunds on digital downloads (industry standard)
