Group Therapy in Cumming, Georgia

Sometimes the most healing words are, “me too.” Group therapy offers support, insight, and practical growth in a professionally guided space where you do not have to carry everything alone.

Request an Appointment View All Services

What to expect from group therapy

  • Therapist-led sessions with structure, boundaries, and clear group agreements.
  • A supportive space where shared experiences reduce isolation and shame.
  • Practical strategies and reflection you can carry into daily life between sessions.
  • Different themes based on current offerings and community needs.
  • A chance to grow relational skills in real time with guided support.

Interested in individual support while waiting for a group? Explore Anxiety Therapy, Trauma Therapy, or Couples Therapy.
