Couples Therapy in Cumming, Georgia

Sometimes a relationship is not in crisis so much as stuck. You may love each other deeply and still keep having the same painful argument, miss each other completely in conflict, or feel like you have drifted into distance and defensiveness. Couples therapy can help you slow those patterns down and reconnect with more honesty, clarity, and care.

Request an Appointment View All Services

Couples therapy may help if you feel caught in patterns like

  • The same conflict keeps repeating and neither of you feels heard by the end of it.
  • You have become more like logistical roommates than emotional partners.
  • Trust has been shaken and you are trying to figure out what repair would actually require.
  • One or both of you tends to withdraw, escalate, shut down, or pursue when things feel tense.
  • You want to strengthen communication, connection, or premarital clarity before resentment grows.

If individual anxiety, trauma, or OCD is putting pressure on your relationship, it may also help to explore Anxiety Therapy, OCD Therapy and ERP, or Trauma Therapy.

Meet the therapist who offers this service

Couples therapy at Fern & Feather is offered by Nicole Browning.

Nicole Browning

PhD, LPC | Co-Founder

Nicole works with couples who want to communicate more clearly, repair trust, reconnect emotionally, and better understand the patterns that keep them stuck. Her style is warm, direct, collaborative, and deeply informed by both science and relationship dynamics.

Areas of emphasis: attachment-focused couples work, communication and conflict patterns, nervous-system awareness, relationship repair, and Gottman Method-informed tools used in a thoughtful, non-formulaic way.
