Trauma Therapy in Cumming, Georgia

Trauma can shape the way you think, feel, relate, and move through the world long after the original experience is over. You might feel constantly braced, emotionally flooded, numb, disconnected, ashamed, or like your body never fully got the message that the danger has passed. Therapy can help you build more safety, flexibility, and self-trust.

Request an Appointment View All Services

Trauma can linger in ways that are hard to explain

  • You feel hypervigilant, easily startled, or unable to relax even when things seem “fine.”
  • You swing between overwhelm and shutdown, or you feel disconnected from your body, emotions, or sense of self.
  • You carry shame, self-blame, or relationship patterns that trace back to what you have survived.
  • You keep functioning on the outside while your nervous system stays stuck in survival mode underneath it all.
  • You know insight alone has not been enough to help your body feel safer or more settled.

If trauma and intrusive thoughts overlap for you, OCD Therapy may also be relevant. If your main struggle feels more like panic, overthinking, or chronic worry, Anxiety Therapy may be a better starting point.
