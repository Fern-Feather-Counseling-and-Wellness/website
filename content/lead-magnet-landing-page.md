## Problem Section

### Headline
"Your Anxiety Lives in Your Body — Not Just Your Mind"

### Body Copy
You've tried breathing exercises. You've read the self-help books. You've told yourself to "just relax."

But your heart is still racing. Your shoulders won't unclench. You're stuck in fight-or-flight and nothing seems to help.

Here's what most people miss: **Anxiety isn't just in your head. It's in your nervous system.**

When you're anxious, your body is preparing for threat. Your heart rate increases. Your muscles tense. Your digestion slows. These aren't thoughts — they're physiological responses.

Talk therapy helps. But sometimes you need tools that work directly with your body.

That's where somatic techniques come in.

## Solution Section

### Headline
"Introducing: The Somatic Toolkit"

### Body Copy
We've compiled 4 evidence-based, printable tools that help you regulate your nervous system — no special equipment, no appointment, no cost.

**What's Inside:**

✓ **Window of Tolerance Tracker**
Understand your nervous system states. Track when you're in hyperarousal (anxiety), hypoarousal (shutdown), or your optimal zone. Learn what triggers what — and what helps.

✓ **Grounding Technique Cards (10 Cards)**
Portable regulation tools you can keep in your wallet, car, or desk. Each card includes when to use it, how to do it, and why it works. Includes 5-4-3-2-1 senses, physiological sigh, cold water reset, box breathing, and more.

✓ **Mood & Energy Tracker**
Monthly tracking system to identify patterns between your mood, energy, sleep, and life events. Spot correlations you might otherwise miss. Bring it to therapy sessions for deeper insights.

✓ **Somatic Habit Tracker**
Build nervous system regulation into your daily life. Track 3-5 somatic practices weekly, monitor completion rates, and correlate with your overall wellbeing. Make regulation a habit, not an afterthought.

## FAQ Section

### Headline
"Frequently Asked Questions"

**Q: Are these tools a substitute for therapy?**
A: No. These tools are excellent supplements to therapy (or great starting points if you're not ready for therapy yet). But clinical anxiety often requires professional support. If you're struggling, we encourage you to reach out.

**Q: Do I need to print these? Can I use them digitally?**
A: They're designed to print (and we recommend printing the grounding cards to keep with you), but you can also use them on a tablet or fill them out on your computer.

**Q: How do I access the tools after signing up?**
A: You'll receive an email immediately with a download link. The tools are yours to keep forever.

**Q: Will you spam me with emails?**
A: No. You'll receive the toolkit immediately, plus our monthly "Nervous System Notes" newsletter (which you can unsubscribe from anytime). We respect your inbox.

**Q: What if I have questions about using the tools?**
A: Each tool includes detailed instructions. If you need more support, consider booking a consultation with us.
