## Script 1: "Signs You Need Therapy That Nobody Talks About"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"You don't need to be in crisis to need therapy."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"Here are signs you need therapy that have nothing to do with depression or anxiety. One: You can't make decisions without asking everyone you know. Two: You keep dating the same person with different faces. Three: You're constantly busy because stillness feels dangerous. Four: You apologize for existing. Five: You can't remember the last time you felt genuinely happy."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Which one hit? Comment the number."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person looking "fine" but empty
- Middle: Quick cuts of each sign
- End: Hopeful transition to therapy office
- Text overlay: "It's Not Always Obvious"

**Hashtags:** #signsyouneedtherapy #therapyawareness #mentalhealth #selfawareness #therapyjourney

## Script 2: "High-Functioning Doesn't Mean Healthy"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"You're successful, exhausted, and wondering why you're miserable."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"High-functioning anxiety is real. You get the promotions, you pay the bills, you show up for everyone. But inside? You're running on fumes. You can't sleep without melatonin. You have a drink every night to 'wind down.' Your body is screaming but you're too busy to listen. Being functional isn't the same as being well."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Save this if you're the 'strong friend.'"*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person winning at life (trophies, awards)
- Middle: Collapse behind closed doors
- End: Peaceful, sustainable pace
- Text overlay: "Functioning ≠ Thriving"

**Hashtags:** #highfunctioninganxiety #burnout #therapy #mentalhealth #success

## Script 3: "Your Body Is Telling You"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Your body keeps score even when your mind is in denial."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"These physical symptoms can mean your mental health needs attention. Chronic tension headaches. Unexplained stomach issues. Jaw pain from clenching. Always getting sick. Racing heart for no reason. If doctors can't find a cause, consider that your body might be processing what your mind won't let you feel."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Share this with someone who 'doesn't believe in therapy.'"*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person at doctor, tests coming back normal
- Middle: Body map highlighting tension areas
- End: Connection between mind and body
- Text overlay: "Listen to Your Body"

**Hashtags:** #somatictherapy #bodymindconnection #mentalhealth #physicalsymptoms #therapy

## Script 4: "It's Not Just a Phase"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"If you've been 'going through a rough patch' for three years..."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"It's not a phase. It's a pattern. We tell ourselves: it'll get better when work calms down, when the kids are older, when summer comes. But the timeline keeps moving. Here's the truth: life doesn't get easier. We get better at handling it. Or we don't. Therapy helps you get better."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Comment if you've been 'fine' for way too long."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Calendar pages flipping rapidly
- Middle: Person waiting, waiting, waiting
- End: Taking action, making appointment
- Text overlay: "Stop Waiting"

**Hashtags:** #therapy #mentalhealth #selfcare #itsnotaphase #patterns

## Script 5: "The Permission Slip"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"You don't need permission to start therapy. But here it is anyway."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"You are allowed to start therapy even if: nothing traumatic happened, other people have it worse, you 'should' be able to handle it, you've been before, you're not sure what to talk about. You don't need a diagnosis. You don't need a crisis. You just need to want something to change. That's enough."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Save this as your permission slip."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person hesitating, making excuses
- Middle: Permission slip visual with text
- End: Peaceful, decided person
- Text overlay: "You're Allowed"

**Hashtags:** #permission #therapy #mentalhealth #youreenough #starttherapy
