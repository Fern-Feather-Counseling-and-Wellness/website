## Script 1: "What Happens in a First Therapy Session"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Your first therapy session isn't what you think."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"Nobody tells you this, but the first session is mostly paperwork and getting to know each other. Your therapist isn't going to make you cry or dig into your childhood trauma. They're going to ask what brought you in, what you want to work on, and explain how they work. It's an interview — for both of you. You're allowed to not come back."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Save this if you're nervous about starting therapy."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person looking anxious in waiting room
- Middle: Split screen expectations vs. reality
- End: Relaxed conversation scene
- Text overlay: "First Session = Just a Chat"

**Hashtags:** #therapyfirstsession #whattoexpect #therapyexplained #mentalhealth #therapyjourney

## Script 2: "Therapists Don't Give Advice"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Your therapist will not tell you what to do. Here's why."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"If you want someone to tell you whether to break up, quit your job, or move cities — a therapist isn't that person. Our job isn't to give advice. It's to help you understand yourself better so YOU can make the decision. Because the right answer for me isn't the right answer for you. And you knowing what you want? That's the actual goal."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Comment if you've ever been frustrated by this."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person asking "Should I...?"
- Middle: Therapist shaking head gently
- End: Person having realization
- Text overlay: "Therapy ≠ Advice"

**Hashtags:** #therapistadvice #therapyexplained #mentalhealth #selfawareness #therapyworks

## Script 3: "Why Therapy Takes Time"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Therapy isn't slow. Your nervous system is."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"People get frustrated that they're not 'fixed' after a few sessions. But here's the thing — you're not broken, and change isn't a light switch. Your brain literally rewires through repeated experiences. One insight doesn't change a pattern. Ten conversations about the same thing in a safe relationship? That changes things. Therapy works, but it works at the speed of trust."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Share this with someone who thinks therapy should be faster."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Person looking frustrated
- Middle: Animation of neural pathways rewiring
- End: Peaceful, changed person
- Text overlay: "Speed of Trust"

**Hashtags:** #therapytimeline #nervoussystem #brainrewiring #mentalhealth #patience

## Script 4: "The Difference Between Therapy and Talking to a Friend"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Talking to a therapist isn't the same as talking to a friend."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"Your friends love you. They give you advice, share their opinions, tell you what they'd do. A therapist does none of that. We're trained to notice patterns you've never seen. We hold space without making it about us. We don't get tired of your story. We don't gossip. And we don't need you to make us feel better. It's a completely different kind of relationship — and for some things, it's exactly what you need."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Follow for more therapy real talk."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Split screen friend vs. therapist
- Middle: Checklist of differences appearing
- End: Person benefiting from both relationships
- Text overlay: "Both Have Value"

**Hashtags:** #therapyvsfriends #therapyexplained #mentalhealth #therapist #supportsystem

## Script 5: "When Therapy Gets Hard"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"The hardest part of therapy isn't starting. It's week 6."*

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
*"Everyone thinks starting therapy is the hard part. It's not. The hard part is when you've been going for a while, you've told your story, and now the real work starts. You feel worse before you feel better. Old patterns fight back. You want to quit. This is called the 'therapeutic window of discomfort' — and it means it's working. Don't quit in the messy middle. That's where change happens."*

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
*"Save this for when you want to quit."*

**Visual Notes:**  
- Start: Graph showing emotional journey
- Middle: Person struggling in session
- End: Person coming through the other side
- Text overlay: "Don't Quit in the Middle"

**Hashtags:** #therapyishard #therapyprocess #mentalhealth #stickwithit #growth
