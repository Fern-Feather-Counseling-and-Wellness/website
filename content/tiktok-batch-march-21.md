### Concept 2: "When a Client Says 'I Don't Know'"
**Hook:** "Clients say 'I don't know' a lot. Here's what's really happening..."
**Style:** POV of therapist listening, with thought bubbles
**Timing:** 30 seconds
**Visual Concept:**
- Screen shows therapist listening (hands visible, not face)
- Thought bubbles appear:
  - "I don't know" = "I'm scared to say it"
  - "I don't know" = "What if you judge me?"
  - "I don't know" = "I've never been asked this before"
  - "I don't know" = "The answer feels too vulnerable"
- Final text: "Therapy isn't about knowing — it's about exploring the 'I don't know'"
**Audio:** Gentle, thoughtful piano music
**Hashtags:** #TherapySession #TherapistThoughts #ClientTherapistRelationship #TherapyInsights #MentalHealthTips
**Caption:** "I don't know" is often the doorway to deeper work. It's not a dead end — it's an invitation. 💭 #TherapistPerspective #TherapyProcess

## Fern & Feather TikTok Concepts (Professional, Educational)

### Concept 3: "Why Trauma-Informed Yoga Is Different"
**Hook:** "Yoga isn't one-size-fits-all, especially for trauma survivors..."
**Style:** Animation showing differences between regular yoga and trauma-informed yoga
**Timing:** 40 seconds
**Visual Concept:**
- Animation of regular yoga: teacher demonstrating, students mirroring, loud instructions
- Animation of trauma-informed yoga: choices offered, gentle language, modifications available
- Key differences highlighted:
  - Choice vs. command
  - Invitation vs. instruction
  - Modification vs. perfection
  - Safety vs. achievement
- Text: "Trauma-informed yoga prioritizes agency over alignment"
**Audio:** Calming, spacious background music
**Hashtags:** #TraumaInformedYoga #YogaForTrauma #SomaticTherapy #NervousSystemRegulation #FernAndFeather
**Caption:** Trauma-informed yoga isn't about doing poses "right" — it's about creating a safe container for your body to explore movement without pressure. 🧘‍♀️ #TraumaRecovery #YogaTherapy

**Hashtags:** #TherapeuticRelationship #TherapyExplained #ClientTherapistBond #TraumaInformedTherapy #FernAndFeather
