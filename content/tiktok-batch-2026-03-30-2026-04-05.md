## Overview
This batch contains **5 short‑form TikTok scripts** for Nicole’s personal brand (no‑face, CapCut style) and Fern & Feather (professional, educational). Each script includes a hook, key points, a brief science note, and a CTA.

---

### 1️⃣ Script – “The 3‑Second Grounding Reset” (Nicole Personal)
**Hook (0‑5 s):** Quick cut of Nicole snapping fingers, text overlay “Stressed? 3‑second reset!”
**Content (5‑20 s):**
1. Inhale for **2 seconds** (count silently).
2. Hold **2 seconds** – feel tension.
3. Exhale **4 seconds**, imagine stress leaving your body.
**Science (20‑30 s):** Briefly mention that this engages the **ventral‑vagal pathway** and reduces heart‑rate variability within 10 seconds (Lehrer & Gevirtz, 2014).
**CTA (30‑35 s):** “Try it now, tag me @NicoleTherapy and drop a 🌿 if you felt calmer. Link in bio for a free 5‑point somatic toolkit.”
**Hashtags:** #SomaticRegulation #QuickReset #TherapistTips #NoFaceTikTok

### 2️⃣ Script – “Micro‑Movement for Anxiety” (Fern & Feather)
**Hook:** Fast‑forward clip of someone tapping foot, text “Anxiety? Move it out!”
**Steps:**
1. Stand, feet hip‑width, knees slightly bent.
2. Do **8 small toe taps** right foot, then left foot, repeat twice.
3. Deep inhale, exhale, feel the floor supporting you.
**Science:** “Micro‑movements stimulate proprioceptive input, signaling safety to the brain (Porges, 2020).”
**CTA:** “Do this in the office or bathroom, comment ‘done’ if you tried it. More hacks in bio.”
**Hashtags:** #AnxietyHack #MicroMovement #TherapyTools #FernAndFeather

### 3️⃣ Script – “5‑4‑3‑2‑1 Sensory Scan” (Nicole Personal)
**Hook:** Text overlay “Overwhelmed? Reset with your senses!”
**Instructions:**
- Spot **5** things you can see.
- Touch **4** things.
- Hear **3** sounds.
- Smell **2** scents.
- Taste **1** thing (maybe a sip of water).
**Science:** “Grounding engages multiple sensory pathways, pulling attention out of threat circuits (Kabat‑Zinn, 2015).”
**CTA:** “Try it now, reply with 👍 if you felt more present.”
**Hashtags:** #Grounding #SensoryScan #TherapistTips #NoFaceTikTok

### 4️⃣ Script – “Box Breathing for Calm” (Fern & Feather)
**Hook:** Visual of a box drawing, text “Box breathing = calm in 30 s.”
**Steps:**
1. Inhale **4 s**.
2. Hold **4 s**.
3. Exhale **4 s**.
4. Hold **4 s**.
Repeat **4** cycles.
**Science:** “Activates ventral vagal complex, reduces sympathetic arousal (Lehrer & Gevirtz, 2014).”
**CTA:** “Do 1‑minute box breathing now, tag @FernFeatherTherapy if you feel the shift.”
**Hashtags:** #BoxBreathing #Calm #TherapyTips #FernAndFeather

**Science:** “Attachment‑focused reflection re‑engages the social neurocircuitry, supporting ventral‑vagal regulation (Bowlby, 1969).”
**CTA:** “Comment ‘I’m checking in’ if you’re doing the exercise. More prompts in bio.”
