## Slide 2: Sign 1 — Chronic Exhaustion

**Visual:** Person looking exhausted but with coffee/phone (relatable)
**Header:** Sign 1: Chronic Exhaustion
**Body Text:** 
- It's not just "tired" — it's bone-deep fatigue that doesn't improve with sleep
- Your body is stuck in hypoarousal (freeze/collapse response)
- Energy conservation mode = survival, not laziness
**What to do:**
- Start with 5-minute movement (walking, stretching) not 5-hour naps
- Check for medical causes (thyroid, iron, sleep apnea)
- Therapy helps retrain your nervous system to find the middle ground
**CTA:** Does this resonate? Comment below 👇

## Slide 3: Sign 2 — Irritability at Small Things

**Visual:** Person irritated by everyday noises/situations
**Header:** Sign 2: Irritability at Small Things
**Body Text:**
- Traffic noise, people chewing, lights too bright
- Your threat detection system is stuck ON (hyperarousal)
- Everything feels like a potential threat
- Not a character flaw — a nervous system signal
**What to do:**
- Recognize it as "my system is overwhelmed" not "I'm a bad person"
- Create sensory buffers (noise-canceling headphones, dim lights)
- Practice "orienting" — look around and name 3 safe things you see
**CTA:** Share this with someone who gets it! 💌

## Slide 4: Sign 3 — Brain Fog & Dissociation

**Visual:** Person looking spaced out, not present
**Header:** Sign 3: Brain Fog & Dissociation
**Body Text:**
- Zoning out during conversations
- Forgetting what you were saying mid-sentence
- Feeling "outside your body" or watching yourself from above
- Your system is protecting you by disconnecting
**What to do:**
- Grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 senses)
- Cold water on wrists or face to bring you back
- Therapy to safely reconnect with your body
**CTA:** Save this for when you need it! ⭐

## Slide 5: Sign 4 — Unexplained Physical Pain

**Visual:** Person with tension in shoulders/neck
**Header:** Sign 4: Unexplained Physical Pain
**Body Text:**
- Muscle tension that won't release (jaw, shoulders, back)
- Digestive issues without medical cause
- Headaches that come with stress
- Your body is holding what your mind can't process
**What to do:**
- Gentle somatic exercises (yoga, stretching, shaking)
- Body scanning to identify where tension lives
- Trauma-informed therapy to release stored tension
**CTA:** Which body part holds your stress? Comment below! 👇

## Slide 6: Sign 5 — Emotional Numbness

**Visual:** Person with neutral expression, disconnected
**Header:** Sign 5: Emotional Numbness
**Body Text:**
- Can't feel joy OR sadness — just "meh"
- Watching emotional movies without feeling anything
- Saying "I'm fine" and actually believing it
- Your system has shut down to protect you
**What to do:**
- Start small (notice temperature, textures, smells)
- Reconnect with safe, gentle emotions first (curiosity, appreciation)
- Therapy to slowly expand your window of tolerance

## Carousel Caption

**Main Caption:**
Your nervous system speaks through your body — not just your thoughts. 

If you're experiencing:
• Chronic exhaustion (not just tiredness)
• Irritability at small things
• Brain fog or dissociation  
• Unexplained physical pain
• Emotional numbness

...your nervous system might be dysregulated. 

These aren't character flaws. They're signals. Your body is trying to tell you something.

The good news: nervous systems can be retrained. With somatic therapy, you can learn to recognize these signals and respond with regulation instead of more dysregulation.
