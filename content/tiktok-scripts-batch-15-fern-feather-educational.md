## Script 1: "Why Your Anxiety Makes Sense"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
"Your anxiety isn't broken. It's protecting you."

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
"Your nervous system learned to be hypervigilant because at some point, it kept you safe. Maybe you had to anticipate a parent's mood. Maybe you grew up in chaos. Maybe you were the peacekeeper.

Your brain wired itself for threat detection. That's not dysfunction — that's adaptation.

The problem? Your body is still running old software in a new environment."

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
"Healing isn't about fixing yourself. It's about teaching your nervous system that safety is possible now. Follow for more."

**Visual Notes:**  
- Calm, neutral background
- Text overlay: "Your anxiety makes sense"
- Soft lighting, professional but warm

## Script 5: "The Window of Tolerance Explained"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
"Are you hypervigilant or shut down? Here's why."

**Content (3-25 sec):**  
"Inside the window: you're calm, present, regulated.

Above the window: hyperarousal. Racing thoughts, panic, anger, can't sit still. Your body thinks it needs to fight or flee.

Below the window: hypoarousal. Numb, disconnected, exhausted, can't think. Your body thinks it needs to shut down to survive.

Most people with trauma swing between these two extremes — rarely landing in the middle."

**CTA (25-30 sec):**  
"Therapy can widen your window so you have more capacity for life's ups and downs. Follow for regulation tools."

**Visual Notes:**  
- Simple diagram/animation of the window
- Use hand gestures to show above/below
- Clean, educational aesthetic

**Hashtags:** #WindowOfTolerance #TraumaEducation #NervousSystem101 #MentalHealthEducation #TherapyExplained
