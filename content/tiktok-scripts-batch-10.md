The problem? It's protecting you from threats that aren't actually dangerous. Emails. Social situations. Your own thoughts.

We don't fight anxiety. We thank it — then teach it the difference between real danger and false alarms."

**Visual Notes:**

- Text: "Protection, not weakness"  

**Caption:**  
Your anxiety isn't weakness — it's misplaced protection 🛡️ Your nervous system evolved to keep you safe. It's just stuck protecting you from things that aren't actually dangerous. We don't fight anxiety. We retrain it. #anxietyisntweakness #nervoussystem #somatictherapy #anxietyhelp #traumainformed #mentalhealth #evolutionarybiology #selfcompassion
