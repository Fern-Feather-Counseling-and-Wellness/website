## Slide 5: Somatic Techniques for Anxiety

**Visual:** Four simple icons with brief explanations
**Text:**
```
Try These Now:

1. **5-4-3-2-1 Grounding** → Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
2. **Body Scan** → Slowly notice sensations from toes to head without judgment
3. **Temperature Change** → Splash cold water on face or hold something warm
4. **Orienting** → Slowly look around, noticing safe elements in your environment
```

**Caption Continuation:**
*These aren't just distractions — they're biological resets. Cold water triggers the mammalian dive reflex, slowing heart rate. Orienting tells your nervous system "I'm safe here."*
