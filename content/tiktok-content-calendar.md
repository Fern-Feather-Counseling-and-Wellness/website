## Batch Creation Workflow

**Monthly (4 hours):**
1. Review calendar, adjust for current events/trends
2. Film 8-10 talking heads (batch by outfit/location)
3. Create 5-7 text-on-screen videos using CapCut templates

**Weekly (2 hours):**
1. Edit batch filmed content
2. Write captions, research hashtags
3. Schedule or manual post daily
4. Engage 30 min/day with comments/DMs
