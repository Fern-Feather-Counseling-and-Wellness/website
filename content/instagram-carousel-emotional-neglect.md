# Instagram Carousel – Signs of Emotional Neglect in Childhood

**Slide 1 – Title**
"Did you grow up feeling *invisible*?"
*Hook image: silhouette of a child with faded background.*

**Slide 2 – What is Emotional Neglect?**
"When a child’s emotional needs aren’t seen or validated. Not abuse, but the *absence* of nurturing."
*Bullet: No consistent affection, little acknowledgment of feelings.*

**Slide 3 – Sign #1: Hyper‑Independence**
"I don’t need anyone. I learned to survive alone."
*Quote style, with a small icon of a lone figure.*

**Slide 4 – Sign #2: Numbing or Detachment**
"‘I don’t feel much.’ – Emotional flatness to protect against disappointment."
*Visual: muted color palette, frozen water droplet.*

**Slide 5 – Sign #3: Fear of Burdening Others**
"‘I don’t want to be a hassle.’ – Suppressing needs to avoid rejection."
*Illustration of a small hand covering mouth.*

**Slide 6 – Sign #4: People‑Pleasing**
"Constantly seeking approval, because validation was scarce."
*Graphic of a person juggling hearts.*

**Slide 7 – Sign #5: Difficulty Naming Emotions**
"‘I’m fine.’ – Limited emotional vocabulary from never being taught."
*Simple chart with blank emotion words.*

**Slide 8 – Healing Path**
"Therapy can help you *re‑learn* feeling safe, heard, and valued."
*Call‑to‑action: Swipe up to book a free 15‑minute consult."
