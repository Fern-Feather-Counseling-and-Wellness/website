# Instagram Carousel: Signs of Emotional Neglect in Childhood

**Slide 1: Title**
"Emotional Neglect: Hidden Wounds from Childhood"

**Slide 2: Intro**
"When a child's emotional needs aren't met, the impact can be subtle but lasting. Here are key signs to watch for."

**Slide 3: Sign #1 – Chronic Feelings of Emptiness**
"*"I feel like something’s missing, but I can’t name it."*\nPeople who experienced emotional neglect often report an ongoing sense of emptiness or numbness."

**Slide 4: Sign #2 – Difficulty Identifying Emotions**
"Struggling to name or understand your own feelings? Emotional neglect can stunt emotional vocabulary development."

**Slide 5: Sign #3 – Suppressed Needs**
"Tending to ignore your own needs, thinking they’re selfish. Persistent self‑sacrifice can stem from growing up feeling unheard."

**Slide 6: Sign #4 – Over‑Reliance on Logic**
"Using facts and rationality to avoid feeling vulnerable. A coping strategy when emotions weren’t validated."

**Slide 7: Sign #5 – Fear of Vulnerability**
"Avoiding intimacy or deep connections because opening up feels unsafe or uncomfortable."
