## Template 2: Educational Newsletter

**Subject:** Your nervous system isn't broken (it's just talking to you)

**Preview text:** 3 signs your body is trying to communicate + what to do about it

---

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

Let's talk about nervous system communication — because your body is constantly sending you signals, but we're often taught to ignore them.

**Sign your nervous system might be dysregulated:**

1. **Chronic scrolling/boredom** — Not laziness, but hypoarousal (your system is shut down)
2. **Irritation at small things** — Not being "difficult," but hyperarousal (threat detection stuck ON)
3. **Saying "I'm fine" and actually believing it** — Not denial, but dissociation (disconnect from body signals)

**Try this instead:**

**The 60-second body scan** (right now, as you read this):
1. Notice your feet on the floor
2. Feel your back against the chair
3. Observe your breath without changing it
4. Scan for any areas of tension or ease

That's it. No meditation app needed. Just checking in.

**Why this works:** When we notice sensations without judgment, we give our nervous system the message "I'm paying attention" — which often calms the alarm system.

**Want to go deeper?**
Our [Somatic Toolkit Workbook] has 12 body-based practices for anxiety, stress, and trauma recovery.

With care,

Nicole

## Design & Formatting Notes

**Visual style:**
- Clean, professional, calming colors (sage green, cream, soft blues)
- Ample white space
- Minimal images (optional: gentle nature photos)
- Clear hierarchy with headings

**Best practices:**
- Send on Tuesday/Wednesday mornings (highest open rates)
- Keep mobile-optimized (60%+ opens on mobile)
- Personalize with [First Name] merge tags
- Include clear CTAs (1-2 max per email)
- Test subject lines with A/B testing

**Compliance:**
- Include physical address (required by CAN-SPAM)
- Clear unsubscribe link
- Privacy policy link in footer
- Opt-in confirmation language
