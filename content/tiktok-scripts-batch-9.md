## Script 1: "I've Already Tried Therapy and It Didn't Work"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"You've been to 3 therapists and nothing stuck. I know why."*

**Script (45 sec):**

"Most therapy stays in your head. You talk about your childhood. You analyze your patterns. You leave feeling like you understand yourself better...

But Monday morning? Same anxiety. Same reactivity. Same you.

Here's what was missing: your body.

Traditional therapy works top-down. Brain to body. And that's valuable.

But if you have trauma, anxiety, or chronic stress, your nervous system needs bottom-up regulation.

Body to brain.

Somatic therapy doesn't replace talk therapy. It completes it.

So if therapy felt like intellectual exercise instead of actual change, you might have been missing the somatic piece.

Follow for what actually works."

**CTA:** "Save if therapy hasn't worked for you yet" / "Comment 'body' if this resonates"

**Visual Notes:**
- Split screen: traditional therapy (talking) vs. somatic (body awareness)
- Text overlays: "Top-down vs. Bottom-up"
- Show the difference between understanding and experiencing change

**Hashtags:** #SomaticTherapy #TraumaTherapy #TherapyFailed #NervousSystem #MentalHealth #TherapyTips #AlpharettaTherapist

## Script 2: "I Can't Afford Therapy Right Now"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Therapy is expensive. Here's what I tell people who can't afford weekly sessions."*

**Script (60 sec):**

"I get it. I'm not going to pretend $175 a session is pocket change.

But let me ask you something: How much is your anxiety costing you right now?

The sleepless nights. The missed opportunities. The relationships you're not fully in because you're managing your nervous system instead of being present.

Therapy isn't an expense. It's an investment in your capacity to earn, connect, and function.

That said — if weekly is too much, I offer biweekly sessions. I have a sliding scale for a limited number of clients. And I'm building digital courses for people who need tools at a lower price point.

But please don't let money be the reason you stay stuck. There are community mental health centers. Training clinics. Group therapy options.

Your nervous system is worth the investment. You just need to find the right format for your budget.

Follow for accessible mental health tools."

**CTA:** "Save for when you're ready" / "Comment QUESTIONS about sliding scale"

**Visual Notes:**
- Authentic, direct-to-camera delivery
- Text overlay with key phrase: "Your nervous system is worth the investment"
- Show compassion without being salesy

## Script 4: "How Do I Know If Somatic Therapy Is Right for Me?"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"Should you try somatic therapy? Take this 30-second quiz."*

**Script (60 sec):**

"Quick assessment. Pause and answer honestly.

One: Do you understand your issues intellectually but still feel stuck?

Two: Does your body react before your brain catches up? Like heart racing, stomach dropping, before you even know why?

Three: Have you done talk therapy and made progress, but certain triggers still get you?

Four: Do you experience anxiety as physical sensations more than worried thoughts?

Five: Are you curious about your body but also a little disconnected from it?

If you said yes to three or more, somatic therapy was made for you.

Your nervous system is asking for body-based support. Not instead of insight, but in addition to it.

I offer free 15-minute consultations if you want to explore whether we're a fit.

Link in bio."

**CTA:** "Comment your score" / "Save this quiz" / "DM me 'consult' for the link"

**Visual Notes:**
- Interactive quiz format with on-screen questions
- Checkmarks appearing for "yes" answers
- Call-to-action screen with clear next step

**Hashtags:** #SomaticTherapy #IsTherapyRightForMe #TraumaTherapy #NervousSystemHealing #MentalHealthQuiz #TherapyConsultation #AlpharettaTherapist

## Script 5: "What If I Don't Have Trauma?"

**Hook (0-3 sec):**  
*"You don't need trauma to benefit from somatic therapy. Here's why."*

**Script (45 sec):**

"Everyone thinks somatic therapy is just for trauma survivors. It's not.

It's for anyone whose nervous system is dysregulated.

That includes:

Chronic stress from your job. Perfectionism that won't quit. Anxiety that shows up in your body before your mind. Difficulty relaxing even when you have time off. Trouble sleeping. Digestive issues with no medical cause.

Your nervous system responds to pressure, expectations, and modern life — not just trauma.

Somatic therapy teaches you to regulate. To come down from high alert. To actually rest.

You don't need a diagnosis. You don't need a traumatic history.

You just need a body that's tired of being stressed.

Follow for nervous system tools for everyone."

**CTA:** "Save if you thought somatic therapy wasn't for you" / "Share with a stressed friend"

**Visual Notes:**
- List format on screen for the "includes" section
- Show diverse people (not just "trauma survivor" stereotypes)
- Calming, inclusive visuals

**Hashtags:** #SomaticTherapy #StressRelief #NervousSystemHealth #AnxietyHelp #ModernLife #MentalHealthForEveryone #AlpharettaTherapist
