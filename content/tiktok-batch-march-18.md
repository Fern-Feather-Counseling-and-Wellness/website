**Hook:** "Your nervous system has a 'window' — here's what that means"
**Format:** Educational graphic + voiceover (or text)
**Length:** 45-60 seconds

**Script:**
- [0-5s] **Hook text:** "The Window of Tolerance: explained in 60 seconds"
- [5-15s] **Visual:** Simple diagram showing three zones
  **Text:** "Imagine your nervous system has a comfortable zone — that's your Window of Tolerance"
- [15-25s] **Visual:** Highlight "Hyperarousal" zone
  **Text:** "Go above it → Hyperarousal. Fight/flight. Racing thoughts, panic, anger, restlessness"
- [25-35s] **Visual:** Highlight "Hypoarousal" zone  
  **Text:** "Drop below it → Hypoarousal. Freeze/collapse. Numb, disconnected, exhausted, can't think"
- [35-50s] **Visual:** Back to middle
  **Text:** "Inside the window → You can think clearly, feel your feelings without being overwhelmed, respond instead of react"
- [50-60s] **Text:** "Therapy helps you widen that window so you can handle more stress without shutting down or melting down"
- [60s+] **CTA:** "Save this for when you need it ✨"

**Visual notes:** Clean infographic style, simple animation or static graphic with text overlays, calming background music

**Hook:** "The difference between somatic therapy and regular talk therapy"
**Format:** Comparison, educational
**Length:** 40-50 seconds

**Script:**
- [0-5s] **Hook:** "Talk therapy vs. Somatic therapy — what's the difference?"
- [5-15s] **Talk therapy side:**
  **Text:** "TALK THERAPY: Focus on thoughts, beliefs, past experiences through conversation"
- [15-25s] **Somatic therapy side:**
  **Text:** "SOMATIC THERAPY: Focus on body sensations, nervous system, physical patterns"
