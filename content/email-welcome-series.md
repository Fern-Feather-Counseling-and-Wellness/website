### Email 2 – "Your First Somatic Tool" (Day 2)
**Subject:** Try this 30‑second grounding exercise (it works!)

**Body:**
```
Hey [First Name],

I hope you’ve had a chance to skim the "5 Somatic Practices" guide. Let’s dive into the first technique: **4‑7‑8 Breathing**.

1️⃣ Inhale quietly through your nose for **4 seconds**.
2️⃣ Hold the breath for **7 seconds** (notice the pause).
3️⃣ Exhale slowly through your mouth for **8 seconds**.

Why it works: This pattern shifts activation from the sympathetic nervous system to the ventral‑vagal state, lowering heart‑rate variability within seconds (Lehrer & Gevirtz, 2014).

Give it a try right now—press play on the guided audio (link below) and breathe along.

[Play Guided 4‑7‑8 Audio]({{audio_url}})

Feel the change? Let me know how it feels. If you’re curious about how somatic regulation can help with anxiety, trauma, or relationship stress, I’m happy to chat.

Talk soon,
Nicole
```
