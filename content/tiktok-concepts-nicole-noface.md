## Concept 1: "On My Way to Your Session"

**Visual:** Person driving aggressively, yelling at traffic, road rage energy
**Text Overlay:** "On my way to my 9am session with you"

- Person chugging coffee while driving

## Production Notes

**Visual Sources:**
- CapCut stock footage
- Pexels/Videvo free clips
- TikTok green screen feature
- Your own B-roll footage

**Text Style:**
- Bold, easy to read font
- White text with black outline
- Positioned for readability
- Multiple text boxes for dialogue

**Sound Strategy:**
- Use trending sounds from TikTok library
- Match audio energy to visual
- Save trending sounds when you hear them

**Posting Strategy:**
- 1-2 per day to start
- Mix of funny/relatable and educational
- Respond to comments with humor
- Build community through authenticity

**Growth Tactics:**
- Stitch/duet with other therapist content
- Comment on trending videos with therapist perspective
- Use trending hashtags + niche hashtags
- Post consistently (algorithm loves consistency)

---

## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Do:**
- Make fun of yourself, not clients
- Show the reality of being human
- Use humor to destigmatize therapy
- Be relatable and approachable

**Don't:**
- Share identifiable client info
- Mock mental health struggles
- Violate ethics codes
- Punch down
