- Animation of brain with "Amygdala" highlighted (fear center)

- Arrow to lungs: "Breathing ↑" (rapid breathing animation)
- Arrow to muscles: "Muscles tense" (tension animation)

**Hashtags:** #PanicAttack #AnxietyEducation #NervousSystem #BiologyOfAnxiety #FernAndFeather
