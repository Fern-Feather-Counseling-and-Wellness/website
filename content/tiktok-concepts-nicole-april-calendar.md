## Production Workflow

**Weekly (Sunday):**
- Batch create 7 videos in CapCut
- Schedule or save as drafts
- Prepare captions and hashtags

**Daily (Morning):**
- Post at optimal time (11am-1pm or 7-9pm EST)
- Respond to comments for 15 minutes
- Engage with other creators' content

**Tools Needed:**
- CapCut (free)
- Stock footage sources (Pexels, TikTok green screen)
- Trending sound library
- Scheduling tool (optional)
