## Safety & Ethics Notes

**For Political Content:**
- No specific client references
- No identifiable details
- General statements about systems, not individuals
- Document everything

**For Professional Content:**
- Clear disclaimers (not therapy, not advice)
- HIPAA compliance always
- Boundaries clear between personal and professional

**For Both:**
- Block/mute liberally
- Don't engage with trolls
- Report threats
- Have crisis plan if doxxed
