Grounding Through the Senses
The 5-4-3-2-1 Technique
What It Is
A grounding technique that uses your five senses to anchor you in the present moment when anxiety feels overwhelming.
The Science
This practice activates your parasympathetic nervous system, signaling safety to your brain through sensory input.
1
See: Name 5 things you can see right now
2
Hear: Name 4 things you can hear
3
Touch: Name 3 things you can feel
💡 Practice Tips
Use this technique when you feel anxiety building. It works anywhere—in your car, at work, or in a crowded room.
