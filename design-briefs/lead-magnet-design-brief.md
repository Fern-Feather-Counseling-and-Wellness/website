## Brand Identity

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Fern Green:** #2D5A3D (calm, grounded, nature-connected)
- **Feather Gray:** #F5F5F0 (soft, clean background)
- **Deep Forest:** #1A3A2A (headers, emphasis)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Warm Sand:** #E8DFD0 (section backgrounds, callouts)
- **Soft Sage:** #A8C5A8 (highlights, icons)
- **Terracotta:** #C67B5C (CTAs, important notes - use sparingly)

**Text Colors:**
- **Dark Charcoal:** #2C2C2C (body text)
- **Medium Gray:** #6B6B6B (secondary text, captions)

### Typography

**Headers (Serif - Trust/Authority):**
- Primary: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond
- Weight: Bold (700) for H1, Semi-bold (600) for H2
- H1: 32-36pt, H2: 24-28pt, H3: 18-20pt

**Body (Sans-serif - Readability):**
- Primary: Inter, Source Sans Pro, or Open Sans
- Weight: Regular (400) for body, Medium (500) for emphasis
- Body: 11-12pt, Line height: 1.6-1.8

**Accent/Quotes:**
- Script or handwritten: Caveat or Dancing Script (use sparingly for quotes)

---

## Notes for Designer (You)

1. **Consistency is key** — Use the same spacing, font sizes, and element styles throughout
2. **Trauma-informed design** — Avoid overwhelming visuals, keep it calming
3. **Accessibility** — Ensure sufficient contrast (4.5:1 minimum for text)
4. **White space** — Don't crowd pages; anxiety content needs breathing room
5. **Brand cohesion** — Every page should feel like Fern & Feather
