# Somatic Self-Compassion Retreat
## 3-Day Intensive for Costa Rica or Sedona

*A transformative journey combining somatic therapy, trauma-informed practices, and self-compassion work in a restorative setting*

---

## Retreat Overview

**Duration:** 3 days, 2 nights  
**Capacity:** 8-12 participants (intimate group for deep work)  
**Setting:** Costa Rica (jungle/ocean) or Sedona, AZ (red rocks/vortex)  
**Investment:** $1,497-$2,497 per person (depending on accommodation tier)  
**Target Audience:** Adults seeking deep nervous system healing, particularly those with:
- Chronic self-criticism and perfectionism
- Burnout and nervous system dysregulation
- History of trauma seeking embodied healing
- Difficulty with traditional talk therapy
- Desire for integrative mind-body practices

**Prerequisites:** Brief intake call to assess readiness for group somatic work

---

## Retreat Philosophy

### Core Premise
Self-compassion isn't a mindset problem—it's a nervous system capacity. You cannot think your way into feeling worthy of care when your body is braced against threat.

This retreat creates the safety, slowness, and somatic experiences that allow self-compassion to become embodied rather than intellectual.

### The Somatic Self-Compassion Framework

**Day 1: Safety & Grounding** — Establishing nervous system capacity for self-compassion  
**Day 2: Exploration & Integration** — Working with protective patterns through the body  
**Day 3: Embodiment & Commitment** — Anchoring new patterns and creating sustainable practice

### Unique Differentiators
1. **Biology-informed approach** — Grounded in polyvagal theory, interoception research, and trauma neurobiology
2. **Small group intimacy** — Maximum 12 participants ensures individualized attention
3. **Dual facilitator model** — Nicole (somatic therapist) + trauma-informed yoga instructor
4. **Nature immersion** — Location chosen for natural co-regulation properties
5. **Integration support** — 30-day follow-up program included

## Detailed Itinerary

### DAY 1: Arrival & Establishing Safety

**Theme:** "Coming Home to the Body"

---

**2:00 PM — Arrival & Check-In**
- Participants arrive at retreat center
- Room assignment, welcome packet, settling in
- Welcome packet includes:
  - Somatic Self-Compassion workbook
  - Journal and pen
  - Essential oil roller (calming blend)
  - Herbal tea sampler
  - Schedule and map

**3:30 PM — Opening Circle: Intention Setting**
*Facilitators: Nicole + Yoga Instructor*

- Welcome and grounding meditation (10 min)
- Introductions: Name, what brought you here, one word for what you hope to release (30 min)
- Retreat overview and container setting (15 min)
  - Confidentiality agreements
  - Opt-out options for any activity
  - Support resources available
- Partner sharing: "What does self-compassion mean to you right now?" (15 min)

**4:30 PM — Gentle Somatic Yoga: Arriving in the Body**
*Facilitator: Yoga Instructor*

- 60-minute trauma-informed practice focused on:
  - Grounding through feet and pelvis
  - Gentle spinal movements to release tension
  - Restorative poses supported by props
  - Breath awareness without manipulation
- Emphasis on choice, agency, and internal awareness

**6:00 PM — Farm-to-Table Dinner**
- Family-style meal
- Dietary accommodations honored
- Optional: Intentional eating practice (mindful first bites)

**7:30 PM — Evening Circle: Nervous System 101**
*Facilitator: Nicole*

- Educational session (30 min):
  - Window of tolerance model
  - Signs of hyper- and hypo-arousal
  - Why self-compassion is hard when dysregulated
- Personal mapping exercise (20 min):
  - Participants identify their own nervous system patterns
  - Create personal "early warning sign" list
- Closing grounding practice (10 min)

**8:30 PM — Free Time / Rest**
- Optional: Journaling prompts provided
- Optional: Herbal tea and reading
- Lights out recommendation: 10:00 PM

---

### DAY 2: Deep Work & Integration

**Theme:** "Meeting the Protector Parts"

---

**7:00 AM — Optional Sunrise Movement**
*Facilitator: Yoga Instructor*

- Gentle yoga or walking meditation
- Choice to sleep in instead

**8:00 AM — Nourishing Breakfast**

**9:00 AM — Morning Session: The Neurobiology of Self-Criticism**
*Facilitator: Nicole*

**Part 1: Understanding the Pattern (45 min)**
- Lecture: Why we develop self-critical inner voices
  - Evolutionary function (threat detection)
  - Attachment origins
  - How criticism becomes a protective strategy
- Small group discussion: "What does your inner critic protect you from?"

**Part 2: Somatic Exploration (60 min)**
- Guided practice: Mapping the critic in the body
  - Where do you feel self-judgment?
  - What happens to your breath? Your posture?
- Partner exercise (optional): Witnessing without fixing
  - One person shares, other practices embodied presence
  - Switch roles

**10:45 AM — Break**
- Movement, bathroom, tea

**11:00 AM — Experiential: The Protector Parts Exercise**
*Facilitator: Nicole*

- 90-minute deep somatic process:
  1. Guided visualization to meet the "protector" (inner critic)
  2. Dialogue with the part: What is it afraid would happen without it?
  3. Somatic tracking: Noticing shifts as relationship to part changes
  4. Integration journaling

**12:30 PM — Lunch**
- Silent option available for those processing

**2:00 PM — Free Time / Integration**
- Suggested activities:
  - Nature walk (guided or solo)
  - Journaling by the water/rocks
  - Restorative yoga (self-guided space available)
  - Massage or bodywork (optional add-on)
  - Nap

**4:00 PM — Afternoon Session: Building the Compassionate Body**
*Facilitators: Nicole + Yoga Instructor*

**Part 1: Somatic Self-Compassion Practices (60 min)**
- Hand-on-heart with interoceptive tracking
- Self-havening technique
- Compassionate movement sequences
- Orienting to safety in the environment

**Part 2: Embodied Boundaries (30 min)**
- Physical exploration of saying "no" and "yes"
- Noticing the body's response to boundary setting
- Practice with partners

**5:30 PM — Break**

**6:00 PM — Dinner**

**7:30 PM — Evening Ritual: Fire Ceremony (Outdoor)**
*Facilitators: Both*

- Safe container for release work
- Each participant writes down what they're ready to release (self-criticism patterns, old beliefs)
- Optional sharing before burning
- Closing with grounding and integration

**9:00 PM — Free Time / Rest**
- Optional: Warm bath with Epsom salts
- Optional: Gentle yoga nidra recording provided

---

### DAY 3: Embodiment & Commitment

**Theme:** "Carrying It Forward"

---

**7:30 AM — Morning Yoga: Embodying Compassion**
*Facilitator: Yoga Instructor*

- Heart-opening sequence (gentle, accessible)
- Integration of breath, movement, and self-compassion
- Setting intentions for the day

**8:30 AM — Breakfast**

**9:30 AM — Morning Session: Creating Your Somatic Self-Compassion Practice**
*Facilitator: Nicole*

**Part 1: Personal Practice Design (60 min)**
- Review of techniques learned
- Identifying 3-5 practices that resonated most
- Creating a realistic daily/weekly plan
- Troubleshooting: "What do I do when I can't access self-compassion?"

**Part 2: Resource Anchoring (30 min)**
- Identifying internal and external resources
- Creating somatic anchors for challenging moments
- Practice: "Calling on your resources" visualization

**11:00 AM — Break & Pack**

**11:30 AM — Closing Circle: Integration & Commitment**
*Facilitators: Both*

- Grounding meditation (10 min)
- Sharing: "What am I taking with me?" (30 min)
  - Each person shares one insight, one practice, one commitment
- Facilitator closing words (10 min)
- Group commitment ritual:
  - Each person states their practice commitment
  - Group witnesses and holds the intention

**12:30 PM — Farewell Lunch**
- Celebratory meal
- Exchange of contact information (optional)
- Distribution of take-home materials

**2:00 PM — Departure**
- Checkout
- Optional: Individual check-out conversations with facilitators (15 min each, scheduled)
