# How to Choose the Right Therapist

Finding the right therapist can feel overwhelming, especially when you’re navigating trauma, anxiety, or life transitions. Below is a straightforward, science‑backed guide to help you make an informed decision.

## 1. Verify Credentials and Training
- **Licensure:** Ensure the therapist is licensed in Georgia (LPC, LMFT, Psychologist, etc.).
- **Specialization:** Look for clinicians who list trauma‑informed modalities (e.g., EMDR, IFS, Somatic Experiencing) if that aligns with your needs.
- **Continuing Education:** Check for recent CEU courses on the nervous system, LGBTQ+ affirming care, or trauma work – it signals a commitment to staying current.

## 2. Assess Compatibility
- **Therapeutic Approach:** Ask about their primary modalities. Do they blend science with somatic practices? Do they use evidence‑based CBT, ACT, or integrative methods?
- **Cultural Fit:** Verify that they are LGBTQ+ affirming and respectful of diverse identities.
- **Session Structure:** Inquire about session length, frequency, and any homework expectations.

## 3. Evaluate Logistics
- **Location & Accessibility:** Virtual sessions are available through a HIPAA‑compliant platform; in‑person appointments are offered in Alpharetta.
- **Cost & Payment:** As a private‑pay practice, we provide superbills for out‑of‑network reimbursement. Discuss rates upfront.
- **Availability:** Ask about waitlists and emergency contact options.

## 4. Trust Your Instincts
- **Safety First:** You should feel safe, heard, and respected from the first conversation.
- **Boundaries:** A good therapist will respect your boundaries and seek informed consent for any technique.
- **Feedback Loop:** Therapy is collaborative. Your therapist should welcome feedback and adjust treatment accordingly.

## 5. Take the Free Consultation
- **15‑minute Call:** We offer a no‑obligation, free 15‑minute consultation to discuss your goals and see if we’re a good fit.
- **Preparation:** Bring a brief overview of your concerns and any questions about our approach.

### Quick Checklist
- ✅ Licensed in GA?
- ✅ Trauma‑informed training?
- ✅ LGBTQ+ affirming?
- ✅ Virtual or in‑person?
- ✅ Transparent pricing?
- ✅ Free 15‑minute consultation?
