## Core Identity

### Our Name: **Fern & Feather Wellness Center**
- **Fern** = Resilience, growth, unfurling, nature's quiet power
- **Feather** = Lightness, breath, release, somatic awareness

**The feeling:** Grounded + alive. Scientific + soulful. Structured + spacious.

## Who We Serve (The Ideal Client)

### Primary: The "Newly Woke + Overwhelmed"
- **Stage:** Recently digested hard truths (trauma patterns, queer identity, life transition)
- **Feeling:** Intellectually gets it, emotionally a wreck
- **Needs:** Regulate first → process second → integrate third
- **Why us:** We don't shame the overthinking. We help them land in their body.

### Secondary Segments:
1. **Parents of neurodiverse kids** — desperate for their own regulation
2. **Late-diagnosis adults** (ADHD, autism, queer identity) — rewriting their whole story
3. **Burned-out caregivers/givers** — been holding it together for everyone else

## What Makes Us Different

| Type | Their Move | Their Gap |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Traditional therapy | "Talk it out" | Ignores the body, slow results |
| Wellness influencers | Quick somatic hacks | No clinical depth, toxic positivity |

### Our Edge:
> **"Where Biology Meets Being Human"**

- PhD-level science **plus** clinical skill **plus** embodied practice

## Brand Voice

### We Sound Like:
- That friend who actually read the research and will explain it without being insufferable
- A warm throw blanket with sharp edges
- "I see you, this is hard, here's what actually helps"

### We Do NOT Sound Like:
- Corporate wellness jargon ("synergize your mindfulness")
- Toxic positivity ("just manifest calm!")
- Academics talking to academics
- Therapy-speak without substance

### Vocabulary We Own:
- Nervous system regulation (not "managing anxiety")
- Embodied practice (not "self-care")
- Integrative care (not "holistic")
- Sustainable healing (not "transformation")

## Messaging by Platform

### Instagram/Facebook (Visual + Educational)
**Format:** Carousels, Reels, quote graphics  
**Tone:** Welcoming, smart, visual metaphors  
**Content:**
- "What actually happens to your nervous system when..."
- "Signs you're dysregulated (that aren't just 'anxiety')"
- Quick somatic practices (with the "why" included)

### TikTok (Personality + Justice-Oriented)
**Format:** Talking head, POV, duets  
**Tone:** Sharp, funny, subversive  
**Content:**
- "Therapists who need therapy" relatable content
- Calling out therapy industry BS
- "Your [X] isn't broken, it's protecting you" reframes
- Justice-oriented therapist energy

### Website (Clinical + Warm)
**Format:** Long-form, clear navigation, transparency  
**Tone:** Professional but not sterile  
**Content:**
- Clear service descriptions
- Transparent pricing (no "call for rates")
- Provider bios that sound human
- FAQ that actually answers questions

## Proven Messaging Frameworks

### The "Your Brain Is Doing Its Job" Framework
1. Name the pattern ("racing thoughts at 3am")
2. Reframe as protection ("your brain thinks it's keeping you alive")
3. Offer the regulation practice
4. Normalize the need for support

**Example:**  
"That spiral at 3am? Your nervous system thinks it's a saber-toothed tiger. It's exhausting, but it's not broken. Here's what actually helps..."

---

### The "Terrible Advice" Framework (TikTok)
1. Quote the bad advice ("just stop thinking about it")
2. React (eyeroll, deadpan, wtf)
3. Offer the better way (with science)
4. Soft landing ("it's not your fault you tried the bad advice")

---

### The "We Built This Because..." Framework
1. Name the gap ("couldn't find a space that felt...")
2. What we built instead
3. Who it's for
4. Invite in
