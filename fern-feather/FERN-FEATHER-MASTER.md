## Mission & Positioning

### Mission Statement
Fern & Feather Wellness Center provides LGBTQ+ affirming, trauma-informed integrative wellness for people ready to move from surviving to thriving. We combine rigorous clinical science with embodied practice — because being human is biological *and* emotional.

### Core Values
1. **Science-Based, Not Woo-Woo** — Grounded in research, not trends
2. **Justice-Oriented** — Socially conscious, LGBTQ+ affirming, anti-oppressive
3. **Trauma-Informed** — Nervous system regulation as foundation
4. **Sustainable Practice** — For clients *and* practitioners
5. **Integration** — Biology + psychology + embodiment

### Unique Selling Proposition
> "Where Biology Meets Being Human"

You're not just talking about your feelings. You're understanding your nervous system, your attachment patterns, your embodied experience — with someone who actually reads the primary literature.

### Target Client
- **Demographics:** Adults 25-55, Alpharetta/Atlanta metro area
- **Psychographics:** Values mental health investment ($200+/session), justice-oriented, prefers private pay to avoid diagnosis/insurance constraints, interested in somatic/embodied work
- **Pain Points:** Anxiety, trauma, relationship struggles, life transitions, parenting neurodiverse children
- **Willingness to pay:** Yes — you work with people who see therapy as essential, not optional

| **Why No Insurance** | **How We Make It Work** |
|---------------------|------------------------|
| No diagnosis required for payment | Clear value proposition |
| No third-party interference in treatment | Results-driven marketing |

You're not competing on price. You're competing on value, expertise, and fit. Your PhD — and the rigor it signals — justifies premium positioning.
