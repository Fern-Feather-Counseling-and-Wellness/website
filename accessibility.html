Accessibility Statement

We are committed to making our website and services accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

Last updated: March 2025

Our Commitment

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness is dedicated to ensuring that our website is accessible to all visitors, including those with disabilities. We are continually working to improve the user experience for everyone and applying relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological disabilities.

Accessibility Features

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Descriptive alt text for meaningful images
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Readable font sizes and responsive design for all devices
  • ARIA labels for navigation and interactive elements

Ongoing Efforts

We regularly review and test our website to identify and address accessibility issues. We are committed to making improvements as technology and standards evolve.

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts, some content or features may not yet be fully accessible. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please let us know so we can address it. We are happy to provide information in alternative formats upon request.

Office Accessibility

Our office at 566 Peachtree Pkwy Suite 122, Cumming, GA 30041 is located on the ground floor. If you have specific accessibility needs for in-person sessions, please contact us in advance so we can ensure your visit is comfortable and accommodated.
