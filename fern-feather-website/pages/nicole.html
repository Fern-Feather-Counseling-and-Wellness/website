Meet Nicole

Rooted and Rising

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

Your Guide to a Regulated Life

With a PhD in Biology and training in clinical counseling, Nicole brings a unique and powerful perspective to Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness. She merges her deep understanding of physiological processes with evidence-based therapeutic practices, exploring how our biology intricately shapes our emotional and psychological experiences.

Nicole is passionate about helping clients understand the science behind their stress responses, anxiety, and trauma. She specializes in guiding individuals towards nervous system regulation, empowering them to move from states of overwhelm or shutdown back into their Window of Tolerance.

Her approach is rooted in compassion, curiosity, and a commitment to helping you build resilience, rediscover your innate strengths, and live a more integrated, fulfilling life. She believes that by understanding our biological blueprint, we can more effectively heal and thrive.

Nicole offers both in-person sessions in Alpharetta, GA and telehealth services throughout Georgia.

Specialties

  • Trauma & PTSD
  • Anxiety & Panic
  • Depression
  • Nervous System Regulation
  • Parents of Neurodivergent Children
  • Life Transitions
  • Burnout & Professional Stress
  • Identity & Self-Esteem

Modalities include: Polyvagal-informed therapy, Somatic Experiencing principles, IFS, Trauma-Focused CBT, Mindfulness.
