Identity & Self-Esteem Therapy

Rooted and Rising

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

Discovering and embracing who you are is a lifelong journey. When self-doubt, societal expectations, or past experiences cloud your sense of self, therapy can provide the space to explore, understand, and strengthen your identity and self-esteem.

Exploring Your Sense of Self

Understanding your identity is multifaceted, encompassing:

  • Your sense of self worth and esteem
  • Your personal values and beliefs
  • Self-perception across different roles (professional, personal, relational)
  • Self-discovery and personal evolution
  • Navigating societal expectations vs. authentic self

Our Approach

Authenticity First. We create a safe space to explore who you are without judgment, honoring all parts of your experience.

Holistic Integration: We explore how your thoughts, emotions, body sensations, and life experiences shape your identity.

Building Self-Compassion: Learning to treat yourself with kindness, especially when facing insecurities or self-criticism.

Values-Driven Living: Clarifying what matters most to you and aligning your life with those values.

Specialized Focus: Affirming Identity

We are deeply committed to affirming and celebrating the diverse identities of our clients. Our inclusive approach ensures that exploration of gender identity, sexual orientation, cultural background, and relationship structures are all met with respect and understanding.
