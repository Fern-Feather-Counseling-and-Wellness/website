Grief & Loss Therapy

Rooted and Rising

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

Grief is a natural response to loss, but it doesn't follow a script. The journey through loss—whether it's the death of a loved one, the end of a relationship, a job loss, or a health diagnosis—can be a profound and isolating experience. We are here to walk alongside you, offering support, understanding, and tools to navigate your path toward healing.

Understanding Your Grief

Grief is not just sadness; it can manifest as anger, confusion, fatigue, guilt, numbness, and a disruption in daily life. It's a deeply personal process, unique to each individual and each loss. There's no single "right" way to grieve.

Our Approach

Creating Space for Your Grief: We provide a safe, non-judgmental space for you to express all aspects of your experience—the pain, the anger, the love, and the memories.

Honoring Your Process: We don't rush grief. Our approach is patient and person-centered, respecting that healing unfolds over time and in its own way.

Finding Meaning & Moving Forward: While validating your pain, we also help you explore ways to find continued meaning, reconnect with life, and integrate the loss into your ongoing story.

Types of Loss We Address

  • Bereavement (loss of a loved one)
  • Relationship endings (divorce, breakup)
  • Loss of a job or career
  • Loss of health or physical ability
  • Grief related to life transitions (e.g., empty nest)
  • Loss of a dream or future vision
  • Anticipatory grief
