    Parenting Challenges Therapy

    Rooted and Rising

    Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

    Parenting is perhaps the most demanding role we take on—emotionally, physically, and psychologically. Whether you're navigating the adjustments of new parenthood, managing a neurodivergent child's needs, or feeling lost as your kids become teens, therapy offers space to process, strategize, and reclaim your wellbeing.

    Common Parenting Struggles

    • Postpartum anxiety and depression
    • Parenting neurodivergent children (ADHD, autism)
    • Co-parenting challenges and communication
    • Setting boundaries with adult children
    • Work-parenting balance and guilt
    • Managing your own triggered wounds that parenting surfaces
    • Navigating your child's mental health challenges

    Our Approach

    Regulation before intervention. We help you understand your own nervous system responses to parenting stress so you can show up calmer and more present.

    Practical strategies. Concrete tools for communication, boundary-setting, and behavior management tailored to your family's needs.

    Self-care as necessity, not luxury. Modeling healthy emotional regulation for your children starts with your own wellbeing.

    No judgment. Parenting is hard. All of it. We create space to be honest about the struggles without shame.

    Specialized Focus: Parents of Neurodivergent Kids

    With lived experience and specialized training, Nicole particularly supports parents learning to navigate autism, ADHD, and sensory processing differences—helping you advocate effectively while maintaining your own mental health.
