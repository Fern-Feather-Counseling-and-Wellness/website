    Life Transitions & Adjustments Therapy

    Rooted and Rising

    Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

    Life is a series of transitions—some chosen, some thrust upon us. Graduations, career changes, relocations, marriage, divorce, empty nesting, retirement, or unexpected losses. Even positive changes can destabilize us. We help you navigate these pivot points with clarity, resilience, and self-compassion.

    Why Transitions Are Hard

    Change disrupts our sense of identity, routine, and security. The "in-between" space—no longer who you were, not yet who you're becoming—can feel disorienting and lonely.

    Common experiences include:

    • Grief for what's ending, even when excited about what's beginning
    • Anxiety about the unknown
    • Identity questioning ("Who am I now?")
    • Relationship strain as roles shift
    • Decision paralysis about which path to take

    Our Approach

    Honoring the complexity. We make room for mixed emotions—excitement and terror, relief and grief—without pressuring you to "just be grateful" or "move on."

    Clarifying values. Major transitions offer opportunity to reassess what truly matters. We help you get clear on your priorities before making big decisions.

    Building transition resilience. Skills for tolerating uncertainty, managing the stress of change, and trusting your ability to adapt.

    Creating continuity. Identifying what remains constant and grounding even as circumstances shift.

    Common Transition Points

    • Career changes and professional identity shifts
    • Relocation and cultural adjustment
    • Marriage or commitment ceremonies
    • Divorce and separation
    • Becoming a parent
    • Empty nesting
    • Retirement
    • Health diagnoses
    • Loss of a loved one
