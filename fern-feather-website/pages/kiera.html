    Meet Kiera

    Rooted and Rising

    Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

    Your Partner in Relationship and Trauma Healing

    Kiera brings a deep well of experience and compassion to Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness. With a specialization in trauma work and relationship therapy, she is dedicated to helping individuals and couples build secure, fulfilling connections and heal from past wounds.

    She believes that healing happens in relationship—both with oneself and with others. Kiera creates a warm, non-judgmental space where clients can explore their experiences with curiosity and courage. Her approach is grounded in attachment theory, trauma-informed care, and a profound respect for each person's unique journey toward wholeness.

    Kiera is passionate about empowering clients to understand their relational patterns, develop healthier ways of connecting, and foster greater self-compassion. She is experienced in working with diverse clients, including the LGBTQ+ community and those in various relationship structures.

    Kiera offers both in-person sessions in Alpharetta, GA and telehealth services throughout Georgia.

    Specialties

    • Relationship Issues (Couples & Individuals)
    • Trauma & PTSD
    • Attachment-Based Therapy
    • LGBTQ+ Affirming Care
    • Life Transitions
    • Anxiety
    • Depression
    • Self-Esteem

    Modalities include: Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Gottman Method principles, Internal Family Systems (IFS), Somatic approaches, Attachment-based therapy.
