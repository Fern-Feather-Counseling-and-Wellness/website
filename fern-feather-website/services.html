    Individual Therapy

    One-on-one support for anxiety, trauma, depression, life transitions, and personal growth. We integrate IFS, somatic therapy, EMDR, CBT and other evidence-based approaches to create a tailored plan that fits your goals.

    • Typical length: 45 minutes
    • Common uses: anxiety, trauma processing, relationship work, nervous-system regulation
    • Private pay; superbills provided

    Couples Therapy

    Strengthen communication, repair attachment ruptures, and find practical ways to reconnect. LGBTQ+ affirming and inclusive of all relationship structures.

    • Typical session: 50–80 minutes
    • Common focus: conflict resolution, attachment repair, rebuilding trust
    • We offer structured tools and dyadic somatic practices when helpful

    Group Therapy

    Connect with others who understand. Our trauma-informed groups combine psychoeducation, somatic practice, and peer support in a safe, facilitated environment.

    • Typical group length: 90 minutes
    • Sliding group formats: open groups and closed cohorts
    • Community-focused with clear facilitator boundaries and crisis protocols
