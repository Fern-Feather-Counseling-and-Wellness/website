About Nicole

Nicole is a PhD-trained biologist and licensed clinical counselor (APC). She blends rigorous science with compassionate clinical care — skeptical of oversimplified models, enthusiastic about real change.

She focuses on trauma, anxiety, OCD, and parenting neurodivergent children. She's sharp, direct, and not here to coddle your avoidance.
