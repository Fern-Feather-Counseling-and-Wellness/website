Common Questions

How do I know if therapy is right for me?
If you're struggling and your usual coping strategies aren't working, therapy can help. We offer a free 15-minute consultation.
Do you take insurance?
We're private pay only, which allows individualized care without insurance constraints. We can provide superbills for out-of-network reimbursement.
Do you offer virtual sessions?
Yes! Secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth throughout Georgia.
