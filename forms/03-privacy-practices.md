# FERN & FEATHER WELLNESS CENTER
## Notice of Privacy Practices

**Effective Date:** March 2027  
**Revised:** March 2027

---

### OUR COMMITMENT TO YOUR PRIVACY

Fern & Feather Wellness Center is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your health information. This Notice of Privacy Practices describes:
- How we may use and disclose your protected health information (PHI)
- Your privacy rights
- Our obligations regarding your PHI

We are required by law to maintain the privacy of your PHI and to provide you with this notice of our legal duties and privacy practices.

---

### WHAT IS PROTECTED HEALTH INFORMATION (PHI)?

PHI includes any information about your health, healthcare services you receive, and payment for those services that can identify you. This includes information in your clinical records, session notes, and communications about your care.

---

### HOW WE MAY USE AND DISCLOSE YOUR PHI

The following categories describe the ways we may use and disclose your PHI without requiring your specific authorization:

**1. Treatment**  
We may use and disclose your PHI to provide, coordinate, or manage your mental health treatment. Examples:
- Reviewing your information to prepare for sessions
- Consulting with supervisors or colleagues (without identifying you)
- Coordinating care with other healthcare providers (with your permission)

**2. Payment**  
We may use and disclose your PHI to obtain payment for services. Examples:
- Providing superbills for you to submit to insurance
- Processing credit card payments
- Responding to payment inquiries

**3. Healthcare Operations**  
We may use and disclose your PHI for our internal operations. Examples:
- Quality assessment and improvement activities
- Training and education (with identifying information removed)
- Business planning and administration

**4. Required by Law**  
We will disclose your PHI when required by federal, state, or local law, including:
- Reporting suspected abuse or neglect of vulnerable persons
- Responding to court orders or subpoenas
- Complying with public health reporting requirements

**5. Public Health and Safety**  
We may disclose your PHI to:
- Public health authorities for disease prevention and control
- Law enforcement in specific circumstances (e.g., reporting a crime on our premises)
- Prevention or reduction of serious threats to health or safety

**6. Workers' Compensation**  
We may disclose your PHI to comply with workers' compensation laws.

---

### USES AND DISCLOSURES REQUIRING YOUR AUTHORIZATION

The following uses and disclosures of your PHI require your written authorization:

- Marketing communications (we do not sell or market client information)
- Most psychotherapy notes (separate from general clinical records)
- Disclosures not otherwise permitted by law

You may revoke an authorization at any time in writing, except to the extent we have already acted on it.

---

### YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

You have the following rights regarding your PHI:

**1. Right to Request Restrictions**  
You may request restrictions on how we use or disclose your PHI for treatment, payment, or operations. However, we are not required to agree to all requested restrictions.

**2. Right to Choose How We Communicate With You**  
You may request that we contact you in a specific way or at a specific location (e.g., cell phone only, work address). We will accommodate reasonable requests.

**3. Right to Inspect and Copy Your Records**  
You may request to see and obtain a copy of your PHI. We may charge a reasonable fee for copies. In limited circumstances, we may deny your request.

**4. Right to Request Amendment**  
If you believe your PHI is incorrect or incomplete, you may request an amendment. We may deny the request under certain circumstances.

**5. Right to an Accounting of Disclosures**  
You may request a list of certain disclosures of your PHI made in the past six years (excluding disclosures for treatment, payment, healthcare operations, and certain other exceptions).

**6. Right to Receive a Copy of This Notice**  
You have the right to a paper copy of this notice at any time.

**7. Right to File a Complaint**  
If you believe your privacy rights have been violated, you may file a complaint with:
- Fern & Feather Wellness Center (contact us directly)
- U.S. Department of Health and Human Services Office for Civil Rights

We will not retaliate against you for filing a complaint.

**8. Right to Be Notified of a Breach**  
If there is an unauthorized acquisition, access, use, or disclosure of your unsecured PHI, we will notify you as required by law.

---

### OUR RESPONSIBILITIES

We are required by law to:
- Maintain the privacy and security of your PHI
- Provide you with this Notice of Privacy Practices
- Abide by the terms of this notice
- Notify you if we are unable to agree to a requested restriction
- Notify you of breaches of your unsecured PHI

We reserve the right to change our privacy practices and the terms of this notice. Any changes will apply to all PHI we maintain. We will post a revised notice in our office and on our website.
