### CANCELLATION & RESCHEDULING POLICY

**24-Hour Notice Required:** If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please provide at least 24 hours notice.

**Late Cancellations/No-Shows:** Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice or missed without notice will be charged the full session fee. This fee is not covered by insurance.

**Exceptions:** We understand emergencies happen. Late cancellation fees may be waived in cases of:
- Medical emergency (yours or immediate family)
- Severe weather/travel conditions
- Other circumstances at the therapist's discretion

**Repeated Cancellations:** If cancellation becomes a pattern, we may need to discuss whether weekly therapy is appropriate at this time or whether you would be better served by a different level of care.

### INSURANCE

**Private Pay Practice:** Fern & Feather Wellness Center is a private-pay practice. We do not bill insurance directly or serve as in-network providers.

**Superbills for Reimbursement:** Upon request, we can provide a superbill (itemized receipt) that you may submit to your insurance company for out-of-network reimbursement. Please note:
- You are responsible for understanding your out-of-network benefits
- We cannot guarantee your insurance will provide reimbursement
- Some insurance plans require pre-authorization for out-of-network mental health services
- Reimbursement rates vary by plan
- You are responsible for paying our full fee regardless of insurance reimbursement

**To check your out-of-network benefits, ask your insurance company:**
1. Do I have out-of-network mental health benefits?
2. What is my out-of-network deductible, and has it been met?
3. What percentage of the session fee will be reimbursed?
4. Do I need pre-authorization?
5. What is the process for submitting claims?

**Good Faith Estimate (No Surprises Act):** Under the No Surprises Act, you have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate for the expected cost of services. Please request this if you would like an estimate of your total expected costs.
