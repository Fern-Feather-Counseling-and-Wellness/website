## The 7 Revenue Streams

### 1. **"Nervous System Parenting" Digital Course** 🎯 HIGHEST PRIORITY
**Target:** Parents of neurodivergent children  
**Market Gap:** Enormous. Current resources are either behaviorist/compliance-based OR too "woo" without scientific backing. Nothing bridges biology + practical parenting.

**The Opportunity:**
- 1 in 36 children diagnosed with autism (CDC 2023)
- Parents desperate for regulation strategies that work
- Existing courses focus on "fixing" the child; none focus on PARENT nervous system regulation as the intervention
- Your unique angle: "Your regulated nervous system IS the intervention"

**Product:**
- 6-module self-paced course
- Video lessons (15-20 min each) + downloadable worksheets
- Bonus: "Emergency Regulation Toolkit" (quick audio guides for meltdown moments)
- Community access (optional upsell)

**Evidence Base:**
- Polyvagal Theory (Porges) — framed as clinical heuristic
- Interpersonal Neurobiology (Siegel)
- Co-regulation research (Tronick, Beebe)
- Somatic Experiencing principles adapted for parenting

**Pricing:** $297-$497  
**Projected Sales:** 200-400 units/year = $59,400-$198,800

**Why It Wins:**
- Addresses YOUR specific clinical niche (parents of neurodiverse kids)
- No direct competition with scientific credibility
- High emotional urgency = willingness to pay
- Evergreen content (doesn't require constant updates)

### 2. **"Attachment Biology" Consumer Course + Workbook Bundle**

**Product:**

- "Earned Secure" progress tracker
- Bonus: Partner communication scripts

**Evidence Base:**
- Bowlby/Ainsworth foundational research
- 2024 scoping review on earned secure attachment (Fraley)
- Neurobiology of attachment (Schore)
- Somatic interventions for attachment repair

**Pricing:** $197-$397  
**Projected Sales:** 300-500 units/year = $59,100-$198,500

**Why It Wins:**
- "Where Biology Meets Being Human" — your exact brand
- Differentiated from Attachment Project's generic approach
- Appeals to educated, science-minded consumers
- Natural upsell from lead magnet

### 3. **"Somatic IFS Toolkit for Therapists" (B2B)** 🎯 HIGH MARGIN
**Target:** Licensed therapists wanting somatic integration  
**Market Gap:** Susan McConnell (Somatic IFS) and The Embody Lab exist but are expensive ($597+) and certification-focused. No practical "start using this Monday" toolkit.

**The Opportunity:**
- Current options: Lengthy certifications, high cost
- **Gap:** Practical, immediately applicable somatic IFS techniques without 40-hour commitment
- CE credit potential (high value for therapists)

**Product:**
- 12 technique video demonstrations (5-10 min each)
- Printable session scripts + client handouts
- Case study examples (de-identified)
- Assessment tools for tracking somatic progress
- Optional: 6 CE hours (requires NBCC approval process)

**Evidence Base:**
- IFS research base (2025 scoping review)
- Somatic Experiencing literature
- Integration research (no direct studies yet — opportunity for original contribution)

**Pricing:** $147-$297 (without CEs) / $397-$597 (with CEs)  
**Projected Sales:** 150-300 units/year = $22,050-$179,100

**Why It Wins:**
- B2B = higher willingness to pay
- CE credits = massive differentiator
- Positions you as expert trainer (leads to speaking/consulting)
- Low refund rate (professional development)

## Risk Mitigation

### Ethical Concerns
- ✅ Clear disclaimers: "Educational only, not therapy"
- ✅ No diagnostic claims
- ✅ Crisis resources included
- ✅ Scope of practice respected

### Market Risks
- **Saturation:** Focus on underserved niches (neurodivergent parenting, somatic IFS)
- **Refund rates:** Offer sample lessons, clear descriptions
- **Copycats:** Build brand/personality (hard to replicate)

### Operational Risks
- **Time investment:** Batch content creation, use templates
- **Tech failures:** Reliable platforms, backup systems
- **Legal:** Terms of service, privacy policy, disclaimers
