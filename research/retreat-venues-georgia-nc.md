## Market Intelligence

### Trauma Retreat Trends (2025)
- **Growth area:** Grief & loss specific retreats (3-day women's retreat in Helen GA noted)
- **Format:** 3-day weekend retreats most common
- **Pricing:** BookRetreats.com lists "10 Best Trauma Retreats" — suggests market demand
- **Differentiation:** Most focus on yoga/meditation; few combine with clinical somatic therapy

### Competitive Positioning Opportunity
Fern & Feather could differentiate by:
1. **Clinical depth:** Licensed therapist facilitation (vs. yoga teacher only)
2. **Science-based:** Biology + somatics positioning
3. **Nervous system focus:** Regulation skills over "processing"
4. **Small groups:** 8-12 participants for trauma safety
