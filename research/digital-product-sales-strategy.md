## Executive Summary

**Recommendation:** Start with **Gumroad** for immediate launch, migrate to **Podia** or **Teachable** as you scale, and eventually add **your own website** with Stripe for maximum control.

**Why:** Gumroad has zero upfront costs, handles all tax compliance, and has a built-in marketplace. Perfect for testing demand before investing in infrastructure.

## Platform Comparison

### 1. Gumroad — Best for Starting Out

**Pricing:**
- 10% + $0.50 per transaction (direct sales)
- 30% per transaction (discover marketplace sales)
- $0/month — no monthly fees
- **Tax handling:** FREE — Gumroad is Merchant of Record (handles all sales tax worldwide)

**Pros:**
- Zero upfront cost — pay only when you sell
- Built-in audience/discovery marketplace
- Handles EU VAT, sales tax, compliance automatically
- Simple setup — upload file, set price, done
- Email marketing built-in
- Affiliate program included
- Instant payouts to bank account

**Cons:**
- 10% fee is high for high-volume sales
- Limited customization (can't fully brand)
- No course hosting (just file downloads)
- Basic analytics

**Best for:** Testing products, early sales, low-volume creators

**Fern & Feather Fit:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Perfect for workbooks and toolkits

## Important: HIPAA Considerations

**Good news:** Digital products (workbooks, toolkits) are **NOT PHI** if:
- Sold to general public (not prescribed to specific patients)
- No client data collected during purchase
- No therapeutic relationship established

**What this means:**
- You can sell on Gumroad/Podia without HIPAA concerns
- Standard disclaimers suffice ("for educational purposes, not therapy")
- No BAA (Business Associate Agreement) needed for platforms

**Caveat:** If you ever sell products WITH client data (customized workbooks, 1:1 coaching add-ons), then HIPAA applies and you need compliant platforms (TherapyAppointment, SimplePractice, etc.)

**For now:** Standard e-commerce platforms are fine.
