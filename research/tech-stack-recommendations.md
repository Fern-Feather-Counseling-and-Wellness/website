## Website Builders for Therapy Practices

### Top Recommendations:

**1. Squarespace** ⭐
- **Why:** "Beautifully simple" — top pick for therapists
- **Pros:** Gorgeous templates, build in a weekend, no coding needed
- **Best for:** Professional, aesthetic-focused private practice
- **Cost:** ~$16-49/month

**2. Wix**
- **Why:** 30+ therapist-specific templates
- **Pros:** Built-in Wix Bookings for scheduling, app marketplace
- **Best for:** Need scheduling integration without separate tools
- **Cost:** ~$17-159/month

**3. Avantiy**
- **Why:** AI-driven, one-stop platform
- **Pros:** Free to start, no credit card until publishing
- **Best for:** Tech-averse, want AI assistance
- **Cost:** Free tier available

**4. Webflow** (Advanced option)
- **Why:** Full design control, modern aesthetic
- **Pros:** Pixel-perfect design, fast loading, SEO-friendly
- **Best for:** Want unique, high-end brand presence
- **Cost:** ~$18-49/month
- **Learning curve:** Steeper but worth it for differentiation

## Scheduling & Practice Management

### HIPAA-Compliant Options:

**1. SimplePractice** ⭐
- **Why:** Comprehensive EHR + practice management
- **Features:** Scheduling, treatment plans, claims, payments, client portal
- **Best for:** All-in-one solution
- **Note:** Nicole already familiar with this from Awakenings

**2. Calendly Health Pro Plan**
- **Why:** Now HIPAA-compliant (as of 2025)
- **Features:** Google Calendar sync, automated reminders
- **Best for:** Simple scheduling without full EHR
- **Cost:** ~$19-25/month

**3. Jane App**
- **Why:** Popular with wellness practitioners
- **Features:** Scheduling, telehealth, charting, billing
- **Best for:** Fern & Feather's wellness center model
- **Cost:** ~$79-129/month

**4. TherapyAppointment**
- **Why:** Longest-standing, therapy-specific
- **Features:** HIPAA templates, AI scribe, analytics, self-scheduling
- **Best for:** Established practices

## Ronda's Recommendation for Fern & Feather

**Phase 1 (Launch):**
- **Website:** Squarespace or Webflow (professional, unique aesthetic)
- **Scheduling:** Calendly Health Pro (simple, HIPAA-compliant)

**Phase 2 (Growth):**
- **Upgrade to:** Jane App or SimplePractice for full practice management

**Why:** Start simple and beautiful, then add complexity as needed. Don't over-engineer before launch.
