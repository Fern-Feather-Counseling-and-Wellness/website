## Sliding Scale Philosophy

**Fern & Feather Position:**
- 10-15% of caseload at reduced fee (community care slots)
- Sliding scale: $120-180/session (vs. $200 standard)
- Reserved for: marginalized communities, students, single parents, activists, essential workers
- NOT insurance — still private pay, just accessible

**Financial Impact of Sliding Scale:**
- 2 sliding scale slots @ $140/session = $280/week vs. $400 at full fee
- Weekly reduction: $120
- Monthly reduction: $480
- Annual reduction: $5,760

**Offset Strategy:**
- One additional full-fee client = $800/month covers the gap
- OR: One quarterly workshop = $2,500 covers ~5 months
- OR: Group therapy slots at full rate = more accessible anyway

## Decision Framework

### When to Add a Client?
**Yes if:**
- Current caseload under 12 individual
- Energy/feels sustainable
- Incoming client is ideal fit
- Can maintain 90-min extended sessions as needed

**No if:**
- Already at 12 max
- Feeling depleted
- Would reduce extended session availability
- "Should" rather than "aligned"

### When to Raise Rates?
**Consider raising when:**
- 80%+ of slots filled for 3+ months
- Expenses increased (rent, insurance, etc.)
- Additional training/certification added
- It's been 12+ months since last increase
- Notification: 60 days advance to current clients

**Fern & Feather Rate Increase Schedule:**
- Year 2: $200/session (launch)
- Year 3: $220/session (12% increase)
- Year 4: $240/session (9% increase)
- Year 5: $250/session (4% increase — plateau)
