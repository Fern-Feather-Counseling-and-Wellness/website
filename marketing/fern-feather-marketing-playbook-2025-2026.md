## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Now — March 2027)

### Goal: Build audience, establish authority, create demand before you can take clients

### 1.1 Content Marketing Engine

**The "Science Says" Content Series**
- **Format:** Weekly TikTok + blog post + email newsletter
- **Angle:** "Your nervous system explained by a PhD" — hard science, not woo-woo
- **Topics:**
  - Why anxiety feels like dying (the physiology)
  - What trauma actually does to your brain
  - The attachment styles explained with biology
  - Why EMDR works (the neuroscience)
  - Polyvagal theory: hype or science?

**SEO Blog Strategy**
Target these high-intent keywords:
- "trauma therapist Alpharetta GA"
- "somatic therapy for anxiety Georgia"
- "EMDR therapist near me"
- "nervous system regulation therapy"
- "attachment trauma therapy"

**Content Calendar (Weekly)**
- **Monday:** TikTok video (trending audio + educational content)
- **Wednesday:** Blog post (1,000+ words, SEO-optimized)
- **Friday:** Email newsletter (repurpose blog content + personal insight)

### 1.2 Social Media Presence

**TikTok (Primary Platform)**
- **Posting schedule:** 3-5x per week
- **Content mix:**
  - 40% educational (nervous system, trauma science)
  - 30% myth-busting (therapy misconceptions)
  - 20% behind-the-scenes (your journey to opening)
  - 10% personal (why you do this work)
- **Hashtag strategy:** #traumatherapy #somatictherapy #nervoussystemregulation #atlantatherapist #mentalhealthmatters

**Instagram (Secondary)**
- **Carousels:** Repurpose TikTok scripts into swipeable posts
- **Stories:** Daily behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&As
- **Reels:** Cross-post TikTok content

**LinkedIn (Professional Referrals)**
- **Target:** Other therapists, doctors, wellness professionals
- **Content:** Professional insights, research summaries, practice-building journey
- **Goal:** Build referral network before you open

### 1.3 Lead Generation

**Free Lead Magnet: "5 Somatic Practices for Immediate Anxiety Relief"**
- **Format:** PDF download
- **Delivery:** Email capture on simple landing page
- **Follow-up:** 5-email nurture sequence (already created)
- **Goal:** Build email list of 500+ potential clients before opening

**Landing Page Elements**
- Headline: "Free Guide: 5 Science-Backed Techniques to Calm Your Nervous System in Under 5 Minutes"
- Subhead: "From a PhD Biologist Turned Trauma Therapist"
- Email capture form
- Social proof (testimonials from current practice, if possible)
- Clear CTA

### 1.4 Authority Building

**Podcast Guesting**
Target shows (already researched):
- Therapy Chat with Laura Reagan
- The Trauma Therapist Podcast
- The Hardcore Self Help Podcast
- Where Brains Meet Being Human (your own podcast?)

**Speaking/Workshops**
- Offer free workshops to:
  - Yoga studios (trauma-informed yoga)
  - Corporate wellness programs (stress/burnout)
  - Parenting groups (nervous system regulation for parents)
  - LGBTQ+ centers (affirming trauma care)

**Professional Networking**
- Join Georgia Psychological Association
- Attend local therapist networking events
- Connect with psychiatrists for referrals
- Build relationships with primary care physicians

## Phase 2: Launch (March — June 2027)

### Goal: Fill initial client slots, establish cash flow, refine systems

### 2.1 Soft Launch Strategy

**Waitlist Launch**
- Open waitlist 3 months before first client session
- Offer "founding member" pricing ($150/session for first 10 clients, then increase to $200)
- Email sequence to waitlist building anticipation

**Referral Network Activation**
- Reach out to contacts from networking phase
- Offer "referral partner" status with reciprocal referrals
- Provide professional bio, headshots, and practice info

**Local SEO Blitz**
- Claim and optimize Google Business Profile
- Get listed in Psychology Today, TherapyDen, GoodTherapy
- Submit to local business directories
- Encourage reviews from first clients

### 2.2 Digital Product Launch

**Product 2: "Somatic Interventions Toolkit for Therapists"**
- **Price:** $147-197

**Product 3: "The Embodied Parts Workbook" (IFS + Somatic)**

## What High-Earning Therapists Do Differently

### Key Insights from 2025 Research

**1. They Specialize Narrowly**
- Generalists compete on price
- Specialists command premium rates
- "Trauma therapist" is too broad
- "Somatic trauma therapist for high-achieving women with anxiety" is a niche

**2. They Market Consistently**
- Not just when they need clients
- Content marketing is a long game
- Show up every week, even when full
- Build the pipeline before you need it

**3. They Diversify Revenue**
- Individual therapy has a ceiling (your time)
- Groups, digital products, subleases scale
- Multiple revenue streams = stability

**4. They Price for Profit**
- Average therapy session in 2025: $159 private pay, $111 insurance
- Six-figure therapists charge $200-300+/session
- They raise rates annually
- They don't apologize for their fees

**5. They Invest in Business Skills**
- Marketing, sales, systems, delegation
- They treat practice like a business, not just clinical work
- They hire help (VA, bookkeeper, web developer)
