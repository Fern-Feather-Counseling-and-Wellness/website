Depression Therapy

Rooted and Rising

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

Feeling persistently sad, losing interest in activities, or struggling with low energy can be debilitating. Depression can impact every aspect of your life, but healing and rediscovering joy are possible. We approach depression with compassion, understanding its many facets—from biological factors to life experiences.

Understanding Depression

Depression is complex and can manifest differently in each person. It's often more than just 'a bad mood.' Our approach helps you understand the interplay of thoughts, feelings, body sensations, and life circumstances contributing to your experience. Nicole's background offers a unique perspective on the biological underpinnings of mood states.

Our Approach

Holistic & Evidence-Based: We combine effective therapeutic modalities with a focus on your overall wellbeing—mind, body, and spirit.

Finding Your Light: We work collaboratively to identify sources of hope, build coping mechanisms, and foster resilience.

Gentle Exploration: We create a safe space to explore the roots of depression without judgment, moving at your pace.

What We Address

  • Persistent sadness or emptiness
  • Loss of interest or pleasure (anhedonia)
  • Low energy and fatigue
  • Changes in sleep and appetite
  • Feelings of worthlessness or hopelessness
  • Difficulty concentrating or making decisions
  • Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD)

Tools You Might Learn

  • Strategies for managing low mood and energy
  • Mindfulness and self-compassion practices
  • Cognitive restructuring for negative thought patterns
  • Behavioral activation to re-engage with life
  • Nervous system regulation techniques
  • Developing healthy coping skills
