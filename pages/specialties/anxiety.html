Anxiety & Panic Therapy

Rooted and Rising

Fern & Feather Counseling and Wellness

Anxiety isn't a character flaw—it's your nervous system doing its job too well. Whether you're dealing with generalized worry, panic attacks, social anxiety, or specific phobias, we help you understand the biology behind your anxiety and build practical skills for regulation.

Understanding Your Anxiety

Anxiety often stems from a hyperactive threat-detection system. Through psychoeducation grounded in neuroscience, we help you understand *why* your body responds the way it does—removing shame and building self-compassion. Nicole's background provides a unique perspective on the biological roots of these responses.

Our Approach

Education meets embodiment. We blend cognitive strategies with somatic techniques that work directly with your nervous system.

Practical skills. You'll learn concrete tools for managing acute anxiety, preventing panic, and returning to baseline more quickly.

Pattern interruption. We identify the cycles that keep anxiety stuck and develop personalized strategies for breaking them.

What We Address

  • Generalized Anxiety Disorder
  • Panic Disorder and panic attacks
  • Social Anxiety
  • Health Anxiety
  • Phobias
  • Performance Anxiety

Tools You Might Learn

  • Breathwork and vagus nerve regulation
  • Cognitive restructuring
  • Exposure and response prevention (ERP) principles
  • Grounding techniques
  • Lifestyle and sleep optimization
  • Boundary-setting to protect your nervous system
