Rooted and Rising

Maybe you've always been the responsible one. The helper. The overachiever. The overthinker. The one who keeps everyone else afloat while quietly carrying your own anxiety, perfectionism, trauma, or self-doubt. Maybe you've learned to intellectualize your emotions, research every possibility, or seek certainty because it once helped you feel safe—but now it leaves you feeling stuck.

At Fern & Feather, we specialize in helping thoughtful, curious people step out of survival mode. Through evidence-based, trauma-informed therapy, we help you loosen anxiety's grip, build tolerance for uncertainty, and reconnect with a life guided by your values rather than fear. We offer care in a welcoming, secular, LGBTQIA+ affirming space where diversity is celebrated and every person is treated with dignity and respect.
