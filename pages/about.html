About Fern & Feather

Founded by Nicole Browning and Kiera Smith, Fern & Feather is a women-owned, secular, and affirming counseling practice in Cumming, Georgia. We're passionate about creating a thoughtful, down-to-earth environment where people feel comfortable showing up exactly as they are. We believe people are more than a diagnosis or a collection of symptoms, which is why our approach blends evidence-based psychology with genuine human connection, curiosity, and respect for each person's unique story.

Why We Started Fern & Feather

Fern & Feather was inspired by the idea that growth happens naturally when people have the right conditions. Just like in nature, healing isn't something that can be forced—it unfolds through curiosity, connection, patience, and steady care. We wanted to create the kind of practice we'd want to walk into ourselves: one where you can ask hard questions, understand how your mind works, laugh when you need to, and know you'll be met with both genuine compassion and evidence-based care.

Why "Fern & Feather"? Ferns symbolize deep roots, resilience, and the quiet, steady process of growth. Feathers represent hope, lightness, and the courage to move forward. Together, they reflect the kind of therapy we strive to provide—grounded in science, rooted in authentic connection, and supportive of meaningful, lasting change.

Rooted in healing. Rising into who you're meant to be.

Our Core Values

Like healthy roots, these values support everything we do. They shape how we see people, how we practice therapy, and the kind of space we strive to create every day.

🌿

Whole-Person Care

We believe you're more than a diagnosis or a collection of symptoms. Your experiences, relationships, values, identity, strengths, and environment all matter. Healing begins by understanding the whole person.

🧠

Science

We love the science behind psychology and use evidence-based approaches grounded in research while honoring your unique experiences, goals, and values.

🤝

Collaboration

You are the expert on your own life. We bring clinical knowledge, evidence-based tools, and curiosity. Together, we create a path that reflects your goals-not ours.

🌈

Belonging

Everyone deserves to feel respected, affirmed, and welcomed exactly as they are. We strive to create a space where people can show up authentically without having to shrink themselves or fit into someone else's expectations.

🌱

Empowerment

We believe therapy isn't about fixing people-it's about helping them reconnect with their strengths, build courage, trust themselves, and take up space in the world with confidence and authenticity.

📚

Lifelong Learning

As therapists, college professors, and lifelong learners, we believe growth never stops-for our clients or for ourselves. We continually pursue research, continuing education, consultation, and professional development so we can provide thoughtful, evidence-based care.
