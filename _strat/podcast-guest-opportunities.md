## The Strategy

Podcast guesting = zero-cost marketing + instant credibility + audience building.

**Criteria for this list:**
- Active podcasts with engaged communities (vs. massive download numbers)
- Audience aligned with Fern & Feather values (trauma-informed, justice-oriented, science-curious)
- Hosts who appreciate straight talk over performance

## Nicole's Bio (Short + Long)

### Short Bio (50 words)
Nicole is a trauma therapist with a PhD in biology and over a decade of clinical experience. She's the co-founder of Fern & Feather Wellness Center in Georgia and specializes in somatic approaches to healing. She believes good therapy combines hard science with radical humanity.

### Long Bio (150 words)
Dr. Nicole is a licensed professional counselor (LPC) and trauma therapist with an unusual background: she trained as a research biologist before pivoting to clinical counseling. With over 10 years of experience and dual expertise in cellular biology and somatic psychology, she brings a unique scientific lens to trauma work.

She's the co-founder of Fern & Feather Wellness Center in Alpharetta, Georgia—a trauma-informed, LGBTQ+ affirming practice combining therapy with integrative wellness services. Nicole specializes in somatic approaches, nervous system regulation, and body-based interventions for complex trauma.

She's also a certified yoga instructor and brings embodied wisdom to her clinical work. When she's not in session, she's building digital resources to make trauma education accessible and running her mouth on TikTok about the science of healing. She believes the best therapy is both rigorously evidence-based and deeply human.

- The biology of trauma (nervous system, neuroplasticity, epigenetics)

## Recording Setup (When Booked)

**Equipment:**
- USB microphone (recommend: Blue Yeti or Audio-Technica ATR2100)
- Headphones (to avoid echo)
- Quiet room with soft furnishings

**Tech prep:**
- Test connection 15 min early
- Close all other applications
- Have water nearby
- Put phone on do not disturb

**During interview:**
- Speak slowly (you'll feel sluggish, it'll sound normal)
- Pause before answering (thinking time reads as thoughtful)
- Name-check the podcast and host naturally
- Have your 3 key points in front of you
